The 7 Best Free Karaoke Apps

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Sometimes, all you want to do is let your inner singer out, at least for a few minutes. Other times, you want to take it up a notch and share your singing skills with friends, family, and the whole world.

If you're going to take your singing to the next level, why not start with these awesome karaoke apps?

1. Smule: Sing It Your Way

Close

Smule has been around since 2012, so you know it's the real deal. Smule is one of the best apps to sing a capella on your own or with friends. You can record yourself and then add some special effects to your voice or video with just a few taps.

Smule also lets you get closer to your favorite artists and singers. You can find many big and small artists on the platform, from Ed Sheeran to Em Rossi. And, of course, you can find other keen amateur singers like yourself and interact with them whenever you want. Once you get more comfortable with your singing, you can start using some apps for musicians to record their songs.

Download: Smule for Android | iOS (Free, subscription available)

2. Karaoke by Yokee: Millions of Songs Just for You

Close

If you're thinking of a song you want to sing, chances are Karaoke by Yokee has it. This app has over one million songs for you to choose from and sing to your heart's content. You can choose from classic '80s music to the newest trending tunes. You can also record yourself and add cute and cool effects after the fact.

What's great about Karaoke is that you can quickly share your performance with the whole world. And in case you need some extra inspiration, you can access the app's feed with hundreds of people sharing their performances. You can leave them a comment saying how amazing they are and even follow them so that you know when their next cover is available.

Another cool aspect of the app is that you can sing many songs for free. Karaoke adds free songs on a daily basis. Of course, if you want full access to the latest and greatest hits, you'll need to subscribe.

Download: Karaoke by Yokee for Android | iOS (Free, in-app purchase available)

3. StarMaker: Join a Huge Community

Close

If you don't have the type of friends who like karaoke much, you can use StarMaker to find people who love singing just as much as you do.

StarMaker has a huge community of over 50 million users who enjoy singing and listening to music worldwide. It doesn't matter if you're into rock, pop, or reggaeâ€”you're going to find someone who shares your music taste and who wants to sing with you in a duet.

If you want, you can also go solo and broadcast yourself live for friends and fans to hear you sing your favorite tunes and dance along to them. There's always something to do and someone to do it with.

Download: StarMaker for Android | iOS (Free, in-app purchases available)

4. Simply Sing: More Than Just Karaoke

Close

If you want to take your singing skills to the next level but you don't want to start paying for classes, we got the solution for you: Simply Sing.

If you're into music, that name probably rings a bell. This app was developed by the team behind Simply Piano, one of the best apps to learn to play piano.

From the beginning, this app will tailor your experience according to what you want to achieve. Are you looking to simply practice your singing skills and have a good time? Or do you want to take your singing to the next level? Whatever your goal is, Simply Sing has you covered.

To get started, the app will ask you for a bit of information about yourself, and it'll determine your song range so it can adjust the songs you can access to. Overall, Simply Sing is a great way to sing along to your favorite tunes or practice your singing skills no matter where you are.

Download: SimplySing for iOS (Free, subscription available)

5. Sing Karaoke: Music for Everyone

Close

If you're looking for a straightforward app with tons of different songs and genres to choose from, the Sing Karaoke app might be the one for you.

There are thousands of songs available that will fit your every mood. You can sing rap, pop, rock, and even Latin music. And if you want, you can also record yourself, with or without your friends, singing your favorite songs. You can then save your performance in the app or share it with the world.

Download: Sing Karaoke for iOS (Free, subscription available)

6. The Voice: The Official Karaoke App for Fans

Close

If you like the Karaoke app by Yokee, but you're also a fan of the TV show The Voice, then you need to try its official karaoke app.

The Voice app offers all the fantastic features of Yokee's original Karaoke app, which means you'll be able to find and sing your favorite songs from any genre and artist you love. And you'll also be able to share with a community of over 100 million people who love karaoke and The Voice just like you.

You can also watch thousands of other singers just like you and interact with them in the app. You can even duet with them and create a fantastic cover together. And if you want to explore other types of songs, be sure to check out the best sites to download karaoke music without words.

Download: The Voice: Sing Karaoke for Android | iOS (Free, subscription available)

7. Baby Karaoke: Let Your Kids Join the Fun

Believe it or not, singing is actually helpful for babies. It assists with language development and also helps kids talk earlier and have a better vocabulary.

That's why a good way for kids to learn and have fun is Baby Karaoke. This app lets you play catchy songs that are great for kids, but you'll probably end up loving them, too. Each piece also has cute and well-made animations to keep your babies entertained while singing along.

Download: Baby Karaoke for Android | iOS (Free)

Time to Sing Your Heart Out

Now, there's no excuse for you to hold your inner singer back. Whether you're a professional vocalist or just like to sing for fun, there's something for you in any of these apps. And after you're done covering other artists' music, you can start creating your own.
