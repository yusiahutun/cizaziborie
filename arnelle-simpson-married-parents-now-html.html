Arnelle Simpson became the talk of the town when she took the stands to defend her father O.J. Simpson, not once or twice but three times. 

A hero-turned-villain, O.J. Simpson was an NFL star, actor, and a broadcaster. Before his life fell from the height of stardom, the running back for the Buffalo Bills was estimated to be worth atleast $3 million.

Wiki-Like-Bio

Arnelle Simpson was born on 4 December 1968 in the United States. 

Born to parents O.J. Simpson and Marguerite Whitley, Arnelle is the couple's eldest child. She had two siblings, a brother named Jason L.Simpson and a sister, Aaren Simpson. Aaren passed away at the age of two from drowning.

Arnelle's parents got divorced in 1979. Her father eventually got married to Nicole Brown in 1985. Arnelle’s family now consists of two more half-siblings; a step-brother named Justin Simpson, and a step-sister, Sydney Simpson.

Arnelle Vouches For O.J. Amidst Murder Trial

Arnelle stood by her father’s side when he was charged with the murder of her stepmother, Nicole and her friend, Ron Goldman. She testified for him saying that her father was emotional, and out of control at the time.

Later in 2008, she retook the stands to defend her father - this time for bank robbery charges. The court ruled in favor of the state and sentenced O.J. for the LA robbery. She testified again in 2013 for the same case. She mentioned that her father was a bit drunk while the fiasco took place. 

Arnelle played a vital role in reducing his sentence. O.J. was bailed out on parole on 20 July 2017 and later released on 1 October 2017 at the age of 70.
