10 Effortless Spring Break Outfits to Wear On Vacation

Publish date: 2024-03-26

Spring break is the ultimate excuse for a seasonal getaway. Whether you're planning on relaxing on a beach, visiting a major city, or exploring a new country with close friends, now is the time to breathe easy, slip on a pair of heels, and spend a week living it up in a series of fun, flirtatious looks.

Spring ensembles are definitely location-dependent, but that doesn't mean they can't still be inspired by red carpet looks and street style stars. 

So, if you're ready to show a little skin and live life to the fullest, read on for the best spring break dress outfits.

The Little White Dress

An LWD is to a beach vacation what an LBD is to life in the city: absolutely essential. Romantic details like embroidery or floral appliques keep things sweet, even if you're rocking a very daring, thigh-skimming hemline.

Flouncy Ruffles

A romantic dress with ruffles and a bit of playful volume is definitely one that you'll want in your suitcase for spring break. A staple for brunch and cocktail hour, a flouncy floral dress is versatile but still serves major vacation-mode style.

A Shirt Dress

Another style of spring break dress that's subtly seductive and incredibly versatile is the shirt dress. If you don't have a go-to shirt dress already in your wardrobe, you can thrift yourself an oversized button-down and belt it at the waist.

This look can easily be dressed up or down, depending on what accessories you choose. Think strappy sandals for an evening of high-end cocktails in a quaint beach town or a cardigan and a pair of Converse sneakers for a night on the boardwalk.

Leather Mini

Weather can be unpredictable on vacation, so if you're headed to a different city, a leather mini should definitely make your packing list. The styling possibilities of a leather mini dress are practically endless, and you can add — or subtract — as many layers as you'd like. Toss on a turtleneck underneath for an overcast day out with friends, or wear it solo for a night out clubbing.

Velvet Sleeves

If you're planning on going out every night of your vacation, you're going to want to pack a few spring break dress outfit options that will work even when the weather is acting up. A velvet minidress with sleeves still counts as fun clubwear but ensures that chilly temps won't keep you from living it up.

A Matching Set

Don't leave home without a matching set tucked safely away in your suitcase. This spring break outfit idea works regardless of your vacation plans, thanks to the playfulness inherent to the matching set. It's free-spirited and fun but still exudes a high level of chicness.

The String Maxi

The string maxidress is womenswear's answer to the string bikini. Daring, sultry, and confident, this is definitely a spring break appropriate 'fit that you might not be able to wear on other vacations. Live it up and remember: The more cutouts, the better.

A Pop of Color

Whether you're jetting to Paris or kicking back in Miami, your spring break outfit checklist won't be complete without a pop of color. The length and fit of your bright dress matters less than its vibrancy. You can show a lot of leg or keep things more conservative. Just make sure you're serving a bright, eye-catching shade.

Cute Cutouts

Cutouts can be found on all types of dresses, from designer maxis and darling minis to bodycon club 'fits and everything in between. For spring break, regardless of location, a minidress on the cutout sweet side is a perfect piece of clothing to pack. A cutout mini with a delicate printed pattern will seamlessly transition day to night.

A Sheer Maxi

The fact that sheer clothing is having such a major moment means the stars have aligned for you to truly look your best on vacation.
