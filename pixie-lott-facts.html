Early Life and Introduction to Music

Pixie Lott was born on January 12, 1991, in Bromley, London, England. She was introduced to music at a young age and began singing in her church school.

Real Name

Pixie Lott’s real name is Victoria Louise Lott. Her nickname, “Pixie”, was given to her by her mother due to her being a “tiny, cute baby who looked like a fairy.”

Early Career in Music

Lott began her music career by singing in school productions and local festivals. She also attended the Italia Conti Academy of Theatre Arts.

Debut Single

Lott’s debut single, “Mama Do”, was released in June 2009. The song reached number one on the UK Singles Chart.

Debut Album

Her debut album, “Turn It Up”, was released in September 2009. The album was a commercial success and spawned several hit singles.

Acting Career

In addition to her music career, Lott has also pursued acting. She made her film debut in “Fred: The Movie” in 2010.

Strictly Come Dancing

Lott participated in the twelfth series of Strictly Come Dancing in 2014, where she was partnered with professional dancer Trent Whiddon.

Philanthropy

Lott is known for her philanthropic efforts. She has supported various charities, including UNICEF and the Alder Hey Children’s Charity.

Fashion Endeavors

Lott is married to model Oliver Cheshire. The couple got married in 2022. She is currently expecting their first baby.

Songwriting

In addition to singing, Lott is also a talented songwriter. She has written songs for her own albums as well as for other artists.

Awards and Recognition

Lott has received several awards and nominations for her music, including two MTV Europe Music Awards and a Brit Award nomination.

Television Appearances
