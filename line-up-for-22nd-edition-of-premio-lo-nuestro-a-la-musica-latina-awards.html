Line Up For 22nd Edition Of 'Premio Lo Nuestro A La Musica Latina' Awards

Publish date: 2024-02-15

MIAMI (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — The Univision Television Network announced the star-studded lineup for the 22nd edition of "Premio Lo Nuestro a la Musica Latina," television's longest-running and top-rated Latin music awards show. The gala event will be broadcast live and, for the first time ever, in high definition from Miami's American Airlines Arena on Thursday, February 18, 2010 beginning at 8pm ET/PT (7pm Central) on the Univision Network. The awards gala will be preceded by a glamorous red carpet arrivals pre-show starting at 7pm ET/PT (6pm Central).


Performances by:


"Premio Lo Nuestro 2010" nominees as reggaeton superstar Daddy Yankee ("Artist of the Year," Urban Genre)

pop star Cristian Castro ("Album of the Year" and "Male Artist of the Year," Pop Genre)

regional Mexican music diva Jenni Rivera ("Female Artist of the Year," Regional Mexican Genre)

pop sensation David Bisbal ("Song of the Year," Pop Genre, "Video of the Year" and "Collaboration of the Year")

banda music group Banda el Recodo ("Album of the Year," "Group or Duo of the Year," "Banda Artist of the Year," Regional Mexican Genre)

Mexican singer Espinoza Paz ("Male Artist of the Year," "Song of the Year," and "Banda Artist of the Year," Regional Mexican Genre)

salsa singer Luis Enrique ("Album of the Year," "Male Artist of the Year," "Song of the Year," and "Tropical Salsa Artist of the Year," Tropical Genre)

regional Mexican music artist Paquita la del Barrio ("Female Artist of the Year," Regional Mexican Genre)
reggaeton artist, Tito "El Bambino"("Album of the Year," "Artist of the Year," "Song of the Year," Urban Genre)

pop artist Pee Wee ("Video of the Year")


The international superstar Chayanne and multi-platinum recording artist Alejandro Fernandez will take the stage to perform and will also be the recipients of two awards for their Artistic Excellence and Career Achievement. The award- winning singer and actress, Thalia will be this year's recipient of the "Young Artist Legacy Award" and will also perform a medley of her hits.


Other artists and "Premio Lo Nuestro" nominees scheduled to appear as performers or presenters are:

Arcangel ("New Artist of the Year," Urban Genre)

Eddy Herrera ("Tropical Merengue Artist of the Year," Tropical Genre)
El Chapo de Sinaloa ("Album of the Year," "Male Artist of the Year," and "Banda Artist of the Year," Regional Mexican Genre)
German Montero ("Male Artist of the Year," Regional Mexican Genre)
Hector "El Torito" Acosta ("Male Artist of the Year," "Tropical Traditional Artist of the Year," Tropical Genre)

Jorge Celedon and Jimmy Zambrano ("Group or Duo of the Year," "Tropical Traditional Artist of the Year," Tropical Genre)

La Arrolladora Banda el Limon ("Group or Duo of the Year," "Song of the Year," and "Banda Artist of the Year," Regional Mexican Genre)
Luis Fonsi ("Male Artist of the Year," and "Song of the Year," Pop Genre, "Entertainer of the Year" and "Collaboration of the Year")
Luz Rios ("Female Artist of the Year," Regional Mexican Genre)
Melina Leon ("Female Artist of the Year," Tropical Genre)
Tati ("New Soloist or Group of the Year," Pop Genre)

Rounding-up the roster are Mexican artist Ana Barbara and salsa music sensation Victor Manuelle who will host "Premio Lo Nuestro 2010."


Additional performers and presenters will be announced shortly. –by CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
