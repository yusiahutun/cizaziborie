Christina Hendricks vs. Malin Akerman

Publish date: 2024-02-07

It's the battle of the little black leather dress!

Christina Hendricks stepped out last night in this BOSS BLACK by Hugo Boss leather dress at a Tacori Productions event. The "Mad Men" star wore a turtleneck under the dress. A black clutch and heels completed her monochromatic look.

Malin Akerman wore the same dress just one week earlier at the "Nobody Walks" premiere. The "Watchmen" actress -- who recently announced she is expecting -- wore the sleeveless dress as is. She paired the look with colorful Christian Louboutin "Fourmi" slingback sandals to add a pop of color.

So who wore it better?

For us, Malin wins hands down. Christina looks beautiful, but the dress' beautiful boatline neck is lost with her turtleneck. Malin's shoe choice is more inspired as well ... the art deco design adds a fun touch to a flirty dress. You can submit your vote by clicking "Launch Gallery" above.

However, we do have to give Christina props for her awesome clutch, which fits like a glove ... literally. What a cool handle!
