Actor Michael Fassbender is best known for his role as slave driver Edwin Epps in the Oscar-winning film 12 Years a Slave (2013)- an adaptation of American abolitionist Solomon Northup's slave narrative. His portrayal in the critically acclaimed movie also earned Michael an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actor.

Michael is also known for his portrayal of Erik Lehnsherr aka Magneto in X-Men franchise movies.

Age/ Nationality/ Short Bio

Michael Fassbender was born on 2nd April 1977 in Heidelberg, Germany to an Irish mother Adele Fassbender and German father Josef Fassbender.He was raised in small-town called Killarney, in south-west Ireland, where his family moved when he was two years old. His family ran a restaurant in Ireland.

Michael grew up alongside an older sister: Catherine Fassbender, who grew up to be a neuropsychologist.
