JoJo Siwa 'can't wait to have babies' as she learns from her relationship mistakes

Publish date: 2024-02-22

JoJo Siwa has opened up about her desire for love and kids on Raven-Symoné and her wife Miranda Maday’s The Best Podcast Ever.

The Dance Moms star rose to fame at the young age of just 9 years old, and although TLC fans may find it hard to believe, JoJo Siwa recently celebrated her 20th birthday, and can’t wait for the day she can have a family of her own.

JoJo Siwa ‘can’t wait to become a mom’

On the podcast, JoJo, who recently welcomed a new addition to the family, revealed that her biggest dream is to do the Superbowl Halftime performance, and after that, she can “retire and have babies”.

In the personal side of her life, JoJo said from the age of 12, she’s always wanted to be a mom. “I cannot wait to have babies, I want to have so many,” she revealed, to which Raven said she’d be “such a fun mom.”

The 20-year-old went on to say she thinks she’ll be a young mom, however, her becoming mom is a ” lot big process.” Siwa came out as being part of the LGBTQ+ community on social media in January 2021, as Raven and Miranda said they have the same conversation about kids all the time.

She then continued to say she wants to be the one to carry the child when the time comes. At the moment, the Dance Moms star is single, as she said “all she wants” is a romance like Miranda and Raven’s.

JoJo Siwa ‘had to let go’ of her cravings for love

Getting deep on romance, Siwa said romance is “all she wants,” however at the moment she “doesn’t have somebody to love.”

She then revealed she had to let go of her craving as she found herself being “attracted to the first thing, and not having any sort of standards.”

However, the star said she’s “working on that,” and realizes patience is a virtue in these situations.

The Dance Mom star heads back to her reality roots

Siwa recently made a surprise announcement to fans, as she was revealed as one of the celebrity contenders on season 2 of Special Forces: World’s Toughest Test.

She’ll be starring alongside other reality TV giants, including Vanderpump Rules’ Tom Sandoval, and Chrisley Knows Best’s Savannah Chrisley.

Earlier this year, JoJo took a “mystery trip” to New Zealand, as it’s now become clear exactly what she was doing out there. The season premiers on September 25 on Fox, so fans have just over a month to wait to see the star back in action.
