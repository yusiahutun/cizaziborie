US-Based Kenyan Mum Found Dead in Her Bed after Father Unsuccessfully Tried to Reach Her

Publish date: 2024-05-11
  • An anguished family is grieving the loss of their loved one, who allegedly died in her apartment in Gaithersburg city, Montgomery County, Maryland, United States
  • Kenyan woman Linda Bonareri Obinchu is believed to have died between Tuesday, March 14 and Thursday, March 16
  • Her father had tried reaching her, but her phone was off, and her friends engaged law enforcement, who found her dead in her apartment

A family has been thrown into mourning following the passing away of one of their beloved kin.

Linda Bonareri Obinchu was a Kenyan based in Gaithersburg city, Montgomery County, Maryland, United States.

Friends worried over mother

The beautiful woman's day of death was believed to be between Tuesday, March 14 and Thursday, March 16.

According to Diaspora Messenger, the deceased's father was trying to reach her from Kenya.

Gaithersburg woman's phone goes off

After failed attempts to connect with Bonareri, the friends of the deceased contacted and connected with another friend, Ann Joss.

Ann's curiosity led her to her friend's apartment to confirm her safety and the friend's nature, which had worried many people.

Ann sought the help of law enforcement, seeing her friend's door was locked, and no one opened it.

Body released in Baltimore City
