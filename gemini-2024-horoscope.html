This Is Your Lucky Year

Publish date: 2024-09-05

You’re making major strides this year and your Gemini 2024 horoscope is proof that you’re becoming a bigger and better version of yourself. You’ll feel the sparkling shift immediately, as Mercury—your ruling planet—will no longer be retrograde as of January 1, paving the way for you to jump right in to those New Year’s resolutions.

You’re exploring the great beyond this year, thanks to the fact that Pluto—planet of creation and power—is reentering Aquarius from January 20 to September 1, and again on November 19. This will fan the flames simmering in your ninth house of philosophy, adventure, and knowledge, tapping into your desire to see and experience new things. You’re gearing up for a major shift in your perspective, so prepare to see the world in a brand new light.

If you’ve been feeling pressured to fit in to a certain mold or to keep up with the latest trends, 2024 will be the year you break the rules. On February 19, the North Node of Destiny will join forces with Chiron—the wounded healer—in your 11th house of community and global impact, prompting you influence the world in a positive and humanitarian way. Once a lunar eclipse in Libra provokes change in your fifth house of romance and self-expression on March 25, you have the power to truly fall in love (with an idea or an actual person). With a total solar eclipse in Aries soon to follow on April 8, there’s a good change you may be joining forces with a new set of likeminded individuals. Activating your 11th house of networking, 2024 may be the year you find yourself a new team.

By June 2, you should prepare for a radical change in your subconscious mind, as well as the way you process truths in your deepest psyche. As Jupiter joins forces with Uranus in your 12th house of underground thinking, you may find yourself processing your thoughts and emotions in a way you’ve never felt them before. This could even allow you to finally let go of some grudges and pains you’ve been holding onto.

Let it all out, because you’ll want to purge all the inhibiting energy from your being once Jupiter—the Greater Benefic—enters Gemini on May 25. With Jupiter attracting love, abundance, prosperity, and growth in your first house of the self, this transit is bound to amplify your progress and land you in the right place at the right time. Prepare for spontaneous interventions and serendipitous encounters that put you on track for success.

You may be gearing up for a major change in your career and professional status by fall. On September 17, a lunar eclipse in Pisces will spark momentum in your 10th house of publicity, which could lead to an abrupt shift in the way you brand yourself. Whether you’re accepting a promotion or embracing a job change, this transformation has always been a part of your destiny. And once a solar eclipse in Libra lights a fire in your fifth house of creativity and self-expression on October 2, there’s a good chance you may make your true passions the centerpiece of this career shift.

The year wraps up just as Mars retrograde is getting started on December 6. Taking place in your third house of communication and informative exchanges, you may find yourself growing weary with constant notifications and conversations that need answering. Don’t allow socializing to become a chore, because this retrograde wants you to rediscover the passion intermingled with each and every interaction.
