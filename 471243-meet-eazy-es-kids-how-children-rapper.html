Meet Eazy-E's kids: How many children did the rapper have?

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Eazy-E, born Eric Lynn Wright, was an American rapper praised for propelling West Coast rap and gangsta rap. His gangster vision took hip-hop to a higher level and produced songs that defined how people should approach the genre. Unfortunately, Eazy-E did not live to see the fruits of his labour. Even sadder, the rapper passed away, leaving many children behind. But how many children did he have, and where are Eazy-E’s kids today?

TABLE OF CONTENTS

Eazy-E was born on 7 March 1964 in Compton, California, United States. Unfortunately, he felt sick in February 1995 and went to the hospital for a check-up because he had a violent cough. However, doctors diagnosed him with HIV/AIDS, and he later died on 26 March 1995, aged 30.

Who are Eazy-E’s kids?

Before his death, the personality had welcomed 11 children with eight women. As of 2022, his kids have grown up to pursue different careers. So, who are they?

1. Eric Darnell Wright

Eric Darnell Wright, professionally known as Lil Eazy-E, is Eazy-E’s firstborn son. He was born on 23 April 1984 (age 38 as of 2022) in Compton, California, United States. Eric currently works as a rapper and president of Rich & Ruthless Records.

2. Ebie Wright

Erin Bria Wright is the firstborn daughter of Eazy-E and Tracy Jernigan. She was born on 16 July 1991 (age 31 as of 2022). Erin legally changed her name to Ebie. As of 2022, she works as a singer, actress, and entrepreneur.

3. Dominick Wright

Dominick Wright was born on 6 September 1991 (age 31 as of 2022) in Los Angeles, California. His mother’s name is Tomica Woods. Unlike his late father, Dominick chose to become an actor and model. His most famous role was Curtis in the 2015 film Mistaken.

4. Daijah Wright

Daijah Wright is the second-born daughter of Eazy-E and Tomica Woods and Dominic’s sister. She is an American actress, rapper, and singer. Her most popular appearance on TV was in season three of Growing Up Hip Hop: Atlanta. Daijah was born six months after her father’s death.

5. Marquise Wright

Marquise Wright, also known as Yung Eazy, was born on 14 February 1990 (age 32 as of 2022). Marquise followed his father’s path and became a rapper. However, he made controversial remarks that Suge Knight killed his father by infecting him with HIV.

6. Derrek Wright

Derrek Wright, professionally known as Baby Eazy-E or E3, started rapping when he was eight. He has continued with the profession even in 2022. Derrek was lucky to appear with his father in a music video titled Only If You Want It.

7. Henree Cherron Wright

Henree Cherron Wright, professionally known as ReeMarkable, is the daughter of Eazy-E and Kelly Faultersack Robinson. She was born on 13 April 1993 (age 29 as of 2022) in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. ReeMarkable is a rapper and has appeared in Growing Up Hip Hop: Atlanta.

8. Erica Wright

Erica Wright is the daughter of Eazy-E. She was still a child when her father died. Erica seems to be the oldest daughter of Eazy-E because her age is 36 (as of 2022). In 2019, she visited her father’s tombstone to have it changed.

9. Raven Wright

Raven Wright is Eazy-E’s daughter. However, she has kept her life private and does not appear in public.

10. David Wright

David Wright is Eazy-E’s son. But unlike some of his siblings, he has kept his life out of the public domain.

11. Elijah Wright

Elijah Wright is Eazy-E’s son. However, nothing much is known about him.
