15 best-performing primary schools in Nairobi County in 2022

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Nairobi County has 225 public primary schools and hundreds of private primary schools. Because of the many primary schools available, it only makes sense to take your child to the best-performing school in the county. The good news is that, whether private or public, schools in Nairobi perform well at KCPE. It is why Nairobi County ranked seventh after the 2021 KCPE results. But which are the best-performing primary schools in Nairobi County in 2022?

Most of the best-performing primary schools in Nairobi County are private primary schools. That is because the school has many facilities and low student-to-teacher ratios, making it easier for pupils to learn. For example, Makini School, Riara School, and Lakewood School have consistently produced top students in the county and country.

On the other hand, public schools that have performed better over the years include Nairobi Primary School, Hospital Hill Primary School, and State House Primary School.

Which are the best-performing primary schools in Nairobi in 2022?

Nairobi County has some of the best academic giants in the country. Over the years, these institutions have projected their academic prowess in national examinations. Here is a list of the 15 best-performing primary schools in Nairobi County:

1. Happyland Preparatory School

Happyland Preparatory School is a private primary school in Makadara, Nairobi. The institution offers a holistic approach to education and focuses more on academics, sports, music, drama, and the arts.

As a result, the school has seen a steady rise in academic performance in the last few years. For example, in 2021, the top student at the school scored 430 marks at KCPE, while 17 students were on the list of the best 300 students in Nairobi.

2. Hospital Hill Primary School

Hospital Hill Primary School in Highridge, Westlands, Nairobi, is one of the best public primary schools in Nairobi County. It has emerged as the top public school in the constituency and Nairobi County for many years.

Students scored a mean grade of more than 320 marks at KCPE, as was the case in 2014, where students achieved a mean score of 341.53. 2015 was 348, and in 2017 ten out of 200 candidates scored over 400 marks.

3. KBA School

KBA School is a Christian private primary school in Roysambu, Nairobi. The school started in 1991 as an educational ministry of Kahawa West Baptist Church.

Since then, it has grown to become one of the best-performing private primary schools in Nairobi County. For example, in 2021, the top student scored 426 marks at KCPE, and 12 candidates were among the top 300 best students in Nairobi County.

4. Kianda School

Kianda School is a private girls-only primary school in the Westlands area of Nairobi County. Opened in 1977, the school has helped thousands of girls get the best scores at KCPE and become top students in Nairobi County.

5. Lakewood School

Lakewood School is a mixed-day private primary school in Komarock, Nairobi. The institution prides itself on offering the best facilities and environment for all students to learn. As a result, Lakewood School has produced some of the top KCPE candidates over the years.

In the last five years, the school has experienced exceptional performance in KCPE and regional examinations. That is why many candidates gain good marks to join top secondary schools in Kenya.

6. Makini School

Makini School is a top private primary school in Nairobi County and Kenya, starting in 1978. It has campuses in many parts of Kenya that provide an ideal environment for students to learn.

7. Moi Avenue Primary School

Moi Avenue Primary School is one of the best and oldest public schools in Nairobi County. The school, located in Starehe Constituency and founded in 1938, has been a centre of excellence for many years. Although the school has a high student population, it has always been among the top public schools in Nairobi to produce top candidates at KCPE.

8. Moi Educational Centre

Moi Educational Centre is a private primary school in Nairobi West. It provides a diverse and inclusive curriculum that prepares learners to succeed. Furthermore, the school strives to enrich and contribute to the development of learners and ensure their academic progress.

The institution has consistently produced the best students in Nairobi. For example, in 2021, the top student at Moi Educational Centre obtained 429 marks at KCPE, while five others were among the top 100 students in Nairobi County.

9. Mwiki Goodhope Academy

Mwiki Goodhope Academy is a mixed-day private primary school in Kasarani, Nairobi. The school has facilities that help ensure young pupils get the best education. It uses different strategies to educate students, including live interactions, assignments and assessments, and easy access to teachers, which it achieves because of a low student-to-teacher ratio.

In recent times, students who have sat for KCPE at the institution have achieved top marks that have enabled them to join the best secondary schools in Kenya.

10. Nairobi Primary School

Nairobi Primary School is a mixed day and boarding primary school in Dagoretti, Nairobi. It is one of the oldest and best public primary boarding schools in Nairobi County, with a history of producing the best students at KCPE.

The school is one of the most populated in Nairobi, with a student population of over 1,600 annually. Nevertheless, the institution has been among Nairobi's top public primary schools, and its students have consistently achieved higher marks in their final exams.

11. Riara School

Riara School is part of the Riara Group of Schools. It is a private primary school that has succeeded in building a name as a reputable learning institution providing high-quality and holistic education in a well-facilitated environment. Ideally, the school aims to enable students to pass their final exams.

It also focuses on students' intellectual, emotional, spiritual, psychological, and physical development. Academically, the average score at Riara School has consistently remained at 75.7% for 26 years.

12. Rockfields Junior School

Rockfrields Junior School is a private primary school in Donholm, Nairobi, and is part of the Rockfields Schools. The school offers a wide range of academic, technical and creative disciplines that excite students to learn. For many years, Rockfields has maintained a reputation among the best private schools in Nairobi.

As a result, its average mean score has remained around 370 points between 2013 and 2021, thus ensuring its pupils get a position at national and provincial secondary schools.

13. State House Primary School

State House Primary School is a top public primary school in Nairobi County. The mixed-day primary school along State House Road has produced the best learners for many years.

Despite its high population, teachers have done excellent work in educating pupils and nurturing their talents. As a result, most candidates that sit for their KCPE exams at the school get a chance to join top national and provincial secondary schools in Kenya.

14. St. Scholastica Catholic School

St Scholastica Catholic School is a mixed day and boarding private Roman Catholic School in Nairobi County. It is among the best Catholic primary schools in Nairobi, with excellent facilities and high academic standards.

The school provides facilities and an environment that enables quality education to students from all faiths and cultural traditions. Although the school offers the 8-4-4 system of education at the primary level, it also uses the Montessori system of learning at the kindergarten level.

15. Westlands Primary School

Westlands Primary School is one of the best-performing public primary schools in Nairobi County. Despite being a public school, teachers at Westlands Primary always strive to give children every opportunity to achieve their potential.

As a result, the school has consistently ensured that most of its candidates pass their final KCPE exams. However, the school has been excellent in nurturing confidence in children over the years.
