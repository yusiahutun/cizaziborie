I'm Going Braless All Summer in the $16 Bodysuit DDD Shoppers Swear By

Publish date: 2024-07-22

As spring and summer events begin to dominate my calendar, my mind has wandered from what to wear at every occasion to what to wear underneath. My closet is stocked with supportive yet unstylish bras that often come with thick straps and do nothing for the rest of my body. What I’ve been missing is an all-in-one piece that lifts my chest and gives my body a smooth appearance, whether I’m in a fitted or flowy dress, or a pair of shorts and a tank top.


I recently found that in a $16 bodysuit that looks so similar to Skims’ internet-famous option that’s quadruple the price. When I first slipped on the Joyspun Seamless Sculpting Bodysuit, I was most surprised by how much support it offered my perma-saggy chest. So much that I can easily wear it without a bra, not to mention its wireless design makes it feel like second skin. The chest area features various shaping zones to help define your boobs, and it provided a gentle push upward that made my 42-year-old chicken cutlets look like they did when I was in my 20s. 

Joyspun Seamless Sculpting Bodysuit

Around my tummy, the bodysuit’s super stretchy elastic fabric felt flexible, smoothing my waist for a flattering silhouette that wasn’t constricting and didn’t dig into my skin or create any folds. I also love that it has a thong bottom that’s still comfortable, so I can wear anything without worrying about panty lines. And its seamless design — including adjustable spaghetti straps that work well under summer tank tops — is virtually undetectable even when worn with tight clothing. It has a button closure at the bottom so you can easily take it on and off when you need to without needing to undress. 


I grabbed the bodysuit in my regular size, and it shaped me perfectly. While it showed up looking smaller than its fit, it has the ideal balance of stretch and compression to hug my body. I suggest sticking to your typical size versus sizing down, to keep it feeling snug yet comfortable.
