Who Is Chris Coyne? His Bio, Age, Job, Instagram

Publish date: 2024-03-07

You might know Chris Coyne as Let’s Make a Deal star Tiffany Coyne’s husband, but we know him as 2 time Emmy nominee and 1-time MPSE nominee, sound editor, singer, and a songwriter.

Get to know him better as this Chris Coyne Bio proceeds. 

Meet Chris Coyne, Tiffany Coyne Husband

Chris Coyne first met the love of his life Tiffany on a cruise ship. At the time, the duo were employed on the vessel as performers and went on to tour the world together. So, falling in love hard and fast, the lovebirds then exchanged vows on March 27, 2006.

Celebrating their 15th wedding anniversary, Tiffany took it to her IG to share a pic alongside Chris, captioned, “Celebrating 15 years of marriage today with my love! Happy anniversary @chriscoyne1 I’m looking forward to many more amazing adventures together!”

During these 15 years, the duo has had a lot of adventures together. From traveling around the world to sharing the joy of taking their kids to school, the duo admitted that they’ve been lucky in love.

Talking about kids, Chris and Tiffany welcomed their first child, a daughter named  Scarlett Rose, in August 2013. Five years later, the couple then welcome their son Carter Liam in February 2018.

Their daughter, Scarlett joined her 3rd grade in August 2021 — 3 days after her 8th birthday.

As for their son, Carter turned 4 on Feb 25, 2022. He had his first day of pre-school on August 18, 2021. “So proud of this little guy! He had his first day of preschool today and did so great! No tears(from him at least), had fun, and made friends,” Tiffany boasted over her IG.

Also, Tiffany shared that her husband Chris was the best dad ever. “I couldn’t ask for a better Daddy for our little Scarlett,” she once wrote over her IG.

Chris Coyne Age

Chris Coyne was 49 years of age in 2022.

He is 9 years older than his wife Tiffany Coyne who was born on May 6, 1982.

Did you know: Chris’ full name is Christopher B Coyne.

Chris Coyne Job

Chris Coyne is a sound editor, singer, songwriter, stand-up comedian, and modern dancer who has released an album in Dec 2015 called “By God’s Grace.” You can find his album on Amazon for $4.95. It contains 5 songs — By My Side (feat. Davy Nathan), By God’s Grace, I Don’t Want to Fall, Move a Mountain, and Younger Man.

As for his work in the sound department in the entertainment industry, Chris has worked in TV series including Cleopatra in Space (2020), Where’s Wally (2020), Scooby-Doo and Guess Who? (2020), Camp Halohead (2019), Star vs. the Forces of Evil (2018), Big Hero 6: The Series (2018), Transformers: Robots in Disguise (2017), Living with Models (2016), and many more.

Besides working behind the screen, Chris has also appeared on screen in the series Hollywood Safari (1998 – 2001) as “Nick Adams.” Also, he has graced the eps of A Dangerous Place (2012), A 1000 Points of Light (2008), The Last Days of Danny Pelvic & The Thrusters (1998), The Tiger Woods Story (1998), and Power King (1995).

This talented sound editor is also a two-time Emmy nominee and one-time MPSE (Motion Picture Sound Editors) nominee. To be specific, Chris was nominated for a motion picture sound editor’s honors for his work in The Adventures of Puss In Boots: A Savage Place.

As for his wife, Tiffany is a model who shot to fame after appearing on the CBS game show Let’s Make a Deal. Back in the day, she also used to model for both Chase Bank and United Airlines.

Chris Coyne Family

Chris Coyne comes from a family of at least four — his parents Carol A and Richard Coyne, himself, and his siblings.

His mother, Carol turned 78 in October 2021 and often graced Chris’s IG.

Likewise, Chris pops Richard (who turned 78 in August 2022) often appeared on Chris’s socials and always with lovely captions. According to Chris, his father was also a musician who rocked in the band Guitar Rockers. And “he is still rockin’ and rollin’,” to this day, Chris added.

Also, Chris mentioned that his parents were the best grandparents. They always made time for their grandchildren and were very close with the kids.

Talking a little more about Richard, he is from Waltham, Massachusetts. He studied at Watertown Public Schools and later graduated with a communications degree from Boston University.
