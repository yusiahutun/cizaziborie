King Kaka Pens Heartwarming Message to Daughter as She Turns 10: &quot;My Heart&quot;

Publish date: 2024-02-12
  • Rapper King Kaka took to Instagram to celebrate his firstborn daughter Gweth Ombima on her 10th birthday
  • He shared a heartfelt message expressing his pride and love for the young girl whom he referred to as his heart
  • Fans and celebrities commented on Kaka's post, all sending lovely birthday wishes to Gweth

Musician King Kaka has celebrated his firstborn daughter Gweth Ombima as she turns 10.

King Kaka's moving message to Gweth

On his Instagram, Kaka shared a lovely message for the beautiful girl, expressing his love for her.

The rapper said he was proud of her and prayed she had a bright future.

He wrote:

"My heart turns 10 today."
