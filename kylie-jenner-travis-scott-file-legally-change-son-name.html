Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott File to Legally Change Sons Name from Wolf to Aire

Publish date: 2024-02-27

It's official: Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott's son's name will no longer be Wolf.

In documents obtained by TMZ, the former couple reportedly filed documents that requested the legal change of their second child's name from Wolfe Jacques Webster to Aire Webster. TMZ reported that Jenner and Scott both agree with the name switch and have both signed the petition, which is currently going through the courts. The request has not yet been signed off on.

The two "regret the initial name choice of Wolf Jacques Webster," according to the documents obtained by the outlet. "Now that Petitioners have had the chance to spend time with their baby, they believe the name Aire Webster is a better fit."

A few weeks later, however, the Kylie Cosmetics founder announced that they had a change of heart in regard to their son's moniker.
