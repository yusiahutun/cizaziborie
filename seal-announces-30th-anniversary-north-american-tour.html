Seal Announces 30th Anniversary North American Tour

Publish date: 2024-01-30

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Grammy award-winning singer/songwriter Seal announced today (January 18) his 30th Anniversary North American tour.

Produced by Live Nation, he has teamed up with producer Trevor Horn, who has taken on the title of musical director for the upcoming run of shows. In addition, The Buggles will be featured as special guest performers.

The six-week tour run begins April 25 at Phoenix’s Arizona Financial Theatre and will make stops at NYC’s Beacon Theatre and LA’s Greek Theatre. The run ends June 12 at Seattle’s Paramount Theatre.

The 30th Anniversary tour will celebrate three decades since his debut album, Seal (1991), and the follow-up Seal II (1994). The global entertainer will be performing the records in their entirety for the first time with the hit songs, “Crazy,” “Future Love Paradise,” “Killer,” and “Kiss From a Rose,” to name a few.

A fan pre-sale will take place Thursday (January 26), followed by a Live Nation pre-sale on the same day. General on-sale begins Friday (January 27).
