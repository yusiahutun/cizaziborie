Man Buys Elon Musk's Starlink, Dumps Other Network Providers: &quot;It is Working Perfectly&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-24
  • A Nigerian man has happily announced becoming the latest owner of Elon Musk's Starlink network
  • The excited man showed how he unboxed the network and bade goodbye to GLO, MTN and Airtel
  • He offered insights about the Starlink network for those interested in buying one and are asking for the price and how it works

A Nigerian man dumped MTN, GLO and Airtel as he purchased Elon Musk's Starlink network.

"Bye to Mtn, Glo and Airtel Wahala,"@bros_p_ wrote on TikTok.

@bros_p_ shared a clip showing the moment he entered his house with the Starlink network and when he unboxed it.

He also showed how the antenna of the device was positioned in his compound. They had to climb a water tank stand to set up the network.

Cost of Elon Musk's Starlink

Responding to netizens, @bros_p_ revealed he does N38k (about KSh 3700) unlimited data subscription, adding that the network works perfectly.

"If you’re buying from website it’s 460k (about KSh 45k) + shipping. From a retailer like me it’s 550k (about KSh 54k)," he offered.
