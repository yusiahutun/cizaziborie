Where to rent a wedding dress Bridal rental options

Publish date: 2024-03-18

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

My Wardrobe HQ

RENT NOW

My Wardrobe HQ is a popular fashion rental site with an extensive bridal section, where you can both buy and rent secondhand designs. The company was chosen by Carrie Symonds when she married Boris Johnson over the May Bank Holiday, renting a Christos Costarellos gown for her big day. There are also plenty of other well-known bridal designers available to rent at My Wardrobe HQ, including Monique Lhullier, Galia Lahav and Marchesa.

Hurr

RENT NOW

Rental site Hurr recently launched a bridal edit, one which caters to the modern bride with brands including The Own Studio, Charlie Brear, Georgia Hardinge, The Vampire's Wife, Sleeper and Cecilie Bahnsen.

By Rotation

RENT NOW

By Rotation has a great selection of options for brides who want to rent their gown, catering to those who want something classic as well as those who are in the market for something a little different. The app boasts pieces from Jacquemus, Rixo, Rotate, Staud and Victoria Beckham.

Rotaro

RENT NOW

Although Rotaro doesn't have a bridal-specific section, there are plenty of amazing options for brides in the selection, including minimal pieces from The Line By K, voluminous designs by Sister Jane and statement frocks by Cecilie Bahnsen.

Something Borrowed

RENT NOW

If you are after something a little more traditional, look to Essex-based bridal rental boutique Something Borrowed, which has a wide variety of wedding gowns available to rent for your wedding, including designs from Pronovias, Martina Liana and Stella York.

Onloan

RENT NOW

Another great London-based rental company which doesn't cater specifically to brides but will be a great option for those after a low-key look is Onloan, which has pieces from Shrimps, Mother of Pearl, Stine Goya and Maggie Marilyn.
