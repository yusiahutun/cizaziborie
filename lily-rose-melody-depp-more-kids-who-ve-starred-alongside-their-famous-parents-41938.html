Lily-Rose Melody Depp &amp; More Kids Whove Starred Alongside Their Famous Parents!

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Johnny Depp’s 15-year-old daughter, Lily-Rose Melody Depp, is set to star alongside him in the upcoming Kevin Smith film, Yoga Hosers, according to various reports.

Lily, whose mom is Johnny’s former partner Vanessa Paradis, will play a yoga-loving convenience store clerk who battles with her dad’s character, a manhunter.

Though it’s rare to see A-list stars sharing the screen with their kids, it’s definitely not unheard of in Hollywood. Here are 7 more pairs who’ve done exactly that!

Angelina Jolie & Vivienne Jolie-Pitt: Maleficent (2014)

Will Smith & Jaden Smith: The Pursuit of Happyness (2006) and After Earth (2013)

Martin Sheen & Charlie Sheen: Wall Street (1987)

Demi Moore & Rumer Willis: Striptease (1996)

Susan Sarandon & Eva Amurri: Dead Man Walking (1995) and The Banger Sisters (2002)

Sylvester Stallone & Sage Stallone: Rocky V (1990)

Meryl Streep & Mamie Gummer: Heartburn (1986)
