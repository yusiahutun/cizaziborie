Adele's Louis Vuitton tour look

Publish date: 2024-02-10

Adele regularly turns to the biggest names and the best designers for her on-stage wardrobe. In fact, she's almost worn a different brand for every show during her Las Vegas residency; we saw her in Schiaparelli for the opening night, there's been an embellished black Stella McCartney gown, and Harris Reed's first custom design under Nina Ricci, to name but a few. And now, for her latest on-stage look, she added custom Louis Vuitton to her already impressive roster.

The singer's most recent outfit for her 'Weekends with Adele' Las Vegas show was a Louis Vuitton gown designed by creative director Nicolas Ghesquière, and was an incredibly chic choice. Styled by Jamie Mizrahi, Adele's black bustier velvet embroidered gown featured crystal embellishment, dramatic sheer oversized sleeves and a silk organza train.

Courtesy of Louis Vuitton

More than 600 pieces were needed for the intricate embroidery – a of a mix of black sequins, crystals and pearls seen across the bodice and sleeves – and nearly 80 hours of handwork alone.

Courtesy of Louis Vuitton

Courtesy of Louis Vuitton

Adele matched the show-stopping gown – which took more than 400 hours to make in total – with equally as glam hair and make-up. The singer wore her hair in her signature voluminous curls in a classic side-parting, as well as her go-to flicked eyeliner and rosy glow.

The star's Las Vegas residency will run until March, at The Colosseum Theatre at Caesars Palace Hotel, so there's still plenty of time to see many more stylish looks from the singer.

Courtesy of Louis Vuitton

Working alongside her stylist Mizrahi, the pair have commissioned a number of designers to create special one-off pieces for the residency. Adele will wear every design for a whole weekend, which is then archived, rather than changing throughout her performances.
