10 Comfortable Kitten Heel Outfits That Are Stylish

Publish date: 2024-02-26

It's true that a few short years ago, kitten heels were considered questionable — a design that didn't make much sense due to such a teeny, tiny elevation. However, in a post-pandemic fashion world, this specific shoe style seems like the perfect fit: It's just dressy enough without sacrificing comfort or walkability.

While we were momentarily distracted by break-your-ankle platforms, the kitten heel comeback has been sneaking up on us. Not only do street style stars enjoy using this in-between shoe to complete their outfits, but the trend has also been spotted on runways since last season. IRL, kitten heels happen to be incredibly versatile. Since they're both comfortable and elevated, they have the ability to pull double duty, taking the place of either flats or higher heels. Whether you choose to wear them with jeans or a sequined skirt, kitten heels are a playful, unexpected choice that works every time.

Ahead, we've rounded up 10 kitten heel outfits that just might make you fall in love with this slight height-boosting shoe.

With Easy Dresses

Looking for a footwear option to complement your favorite breezy dress? Kitten heels are a small step up from flats. Compared to sky-high stilettos, they're easier for walking, balancing, and wearing throughout the night.

With Loungewear

We may enjoy dressing up, but the truth is, some days call for leggings and loungewear. Kitten heels can make stretchy staples seem sleeker and somewhat fancy — especially if you add something structured to your look, such as an oversized button-down or blazer.

With Timeless Neutrals

Sure, trends work on a rotating basis, but there's a reason this shoe is never "out" for long. Kitten styles are subtle and sweet, but pointed-toe options work to elongate your legs, just like higher heels. Invest in a neutral pair you can hold onto for years to come.

With Sparkly Skirts

They may be small, but these heels still have the ability to make a major statement. Choosing a pair with standout details such as bright colors, embellishments, and sexy straps will complement sequins and other over-the-top pieces while also popping against basics.

With Nostalgia-Inducing Staples

Kitten heels are a known throwback, but while some may associate them with '60s fashion, they were also pretty popular around Y2K — meaning they'll pair perfectly with all the miniskirts, cropped cardigans, and thong pants that have recently resurfaced in our closets.

With Seasonless Pieces

Technically, you can get away with wearing kitten heel sandals year-round, especially when attending formal events like weddings. However, the trend also comes in closed-toe and boot form, which may feel more practical for the fall, winter, and spring months. It's up to you to find a version that will work best for your own lifestyle that also suits your personal aesthetic.

With Formal Officewear

This footwear option is famous for being the ultimate happy medium. Want to keep your outfit from feeling too casual but aren't willing to risk it in platforms? Kitten heels. Need an elevated yet comfortable shoe to go with an elaborate dress? Kitten heels. Depending on the details, they'll add playfulness to your look — or tone it down — and work as a go-to solution for multiple fashion dilemmas.

With Oversized Jackets

A little color coordination goes a long way. Here, burgundy kitten heels echo an oversized matching leather jacket to create a cohesive look appropriate for Paris Fashion Week.

With a Patterned Maxidress

Invest in a pair of neutral-colored kitten heels to incorporate into any ensemble, like this patterned maxidress. Not having to think about whether your shoes match your outfit will also cut down on putting together your OOTD.

With Jeans and a Crisp Shirt

Effortlessly cool, kitten heel sandals are practically made for jeans. When paired with a button-down shirt, you get a style that strikes the right balance between off-duty and sophisticated.
