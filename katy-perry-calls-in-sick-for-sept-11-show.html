Katy Perry Calls In Sick For Sept. 11 Show

Publish date: 2024-10-04

GRAND RAPIDS (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Singer Katy Perry was forced to postpone her scheduled September 11th concert at the Van Andel Arena because of an "upper respiratory illness."

According to the Grand Rapids Press, the show has been rescheduled for December 1st. Live Nation noted that all tickets to the Sept. 11 show will be honored for the rescheduled date and added that there were still a limited selection of tickets available for the show.

Perry iit the road for the most recent leg of her "California Dreams" tour with a performance at the KFC Yum! Arena in Louisville, Kentucky on Sept. 1st and had said on Twitter before that show that she was under the weather.

"Alright childrens! I'm gonna throw myself on stage tonight… snot, sweat, tears and all. Sing loud, I'ma need it!" Perry tweeted before the Lousiville show. – CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
