Publish date: 0001-01-01

The Lipa na M-Pesa service has simplified personal and business transactions in Kenya. The mobile money transfer service offers convenience at a fee. Discover the M-Pesa Paybill charges in 2023 below.

Since its inception in 1993, Safaricom has launched innovative products. One of the most outstanding services is M-Pesa, which has enabled money transfers across several platforms. The Pay Bill service is a cash collection service that allows organizations to collect money from customers through the platform.

What are the M-Pesa Paybill charges in 2023?

Millions of Kenyans use Safaricom's Paybill service to pay bills, buy airtime to other networks, or transfer money to other Safaricom users, people using sim cards from other companies like Airtel, bank accounts, etc. There are short-term Paybills for fundraising.

Safaricom Paybill charges vary with the amount of money you are transacting. To transact via Paybill, use your phone's sim toolkit or the M-Pesa app. Safaricom Limited has three Paybill tariffs with different M-Pesa charges.

How much does Paybill charge per transaction?

Safaricom's Mgao Paybill tariff allows customers and businesses to share charges. Customers pay KES 0 - KES 23, while a business pays KES 0 - KES 82 when customers pay (KES 1 - KES 150,000) under this tariff.

How much does M-PESA charge for Paybill transfer?

Safaricom's business bouquet tariff, all Paybill transfer charges fall on the business. Safaricom charges businesses KES 0 - KES 12 when customers pay (KES 1 - KES 150,000) under this tariff. The customers pay nothing, and this tariff limits the number of business accounts to only one.

What is M-PESA Global?

M-Pesa Global enables registered customers to receive and send money globally. M-Pesa customers can send funds to East Africa (Rwanda, Tanzania, and Uganda, and over 500,000 to millions of bank accounts globally (US, UK, Europe, and UAE) via the Western Union locations and Paypal accounts for online shopping. Registered customers can also receive funds from abroad on their Safaricom mobile money accounts.

What amount can one send through M-Pesa Global?

The minimum amount you can send is KES 101, while the maximum is KES 70,000.
