Accident and work come-back

The journalist is known as a huge lover of horses and horse riding. Even with more than 45 years of experience as a horse-rider, she faced a life-changing accident. On 2nd June 2019, as her horse was racing on the way to goal, she reached too far for a ball that she ended up losing her grip on the saddle.

I knew there was no getting back in place and was sideways on the horse, so I kicked my feet out of the stirrups and bailed off.

The accident was so severe since she was riding so fast and bailed out so hard. She suffered the following injuries:

  • Crushed ribcage
  • Punctured right lung
  • Broken collarbone
  • Broken thumb

The journalist was taken to the BAMC Trauma Unit, where she was kept in the intensive care unit. One day later, the surgeons opened her chest and fixed the broken ribs by adding metal plates for proper amendment.
