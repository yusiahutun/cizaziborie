EastEnders' Danielle Harold tops up tan in tiny bikini as she jets off after Lola axe

Publish date: 2024-01-27

EastEnders beauty Danielle Harold set pulses racing as she shared snaps of her parading her dynamite figure in a tiny black bikini.

The actress - who is best known for playing Lola Pearce in the BBC soap - headed off on a lavish holiday after the British Soap Awards and gave fans a little peek on Instagram.

In one shot, she was posing by a picturesque pool in a plunging black bikini top that left little to the imagination.

Danielle, 30, was clutching sunglasses in her hand as she pouted seductively at the camera with a glamorous poolside backdrop behind her.

Her make-up matched her glam surroundings, with the star sporting a pinkish lipstick and lashings of dark mascara, while her long blonde hair tumbled over one shoulder.

The second image was just of her legs, which looked endless as the star stretched out and soaked up the rays on a lounger.

Another showed Danielle dipping a tanned foot into the water as she perched poolside with a delicious-looking frothy drink beside her.

The actress has played Albert Square's Lola on and off for around 11 years but it was recently reportedly that she was being axed as part of a soap shake-up.

According to The Sun, Dan was not expecting to be shown the door and was "teary" and "in pieces" after hearing that her days in Walford were numbered.

An insider said that the character would be written out of the soap "with no option to return" - which suggests Lola may be killed off.

A source said to the publication at the time: "The cull came out of the blue.

"She was not expecting the chop because her character has been a firm favourite with viewers."

EastEnders airs Mondays to Thursdays at 7.30pm on BBC.
