Samantha Armytage's new man REVEALED as private jet tycoon Paul O'Brien

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Back in June, Samantha Armytage hinted she was in a new relationship when she admitted 'good things' were happening in her personal life.

And on Monday, Woman's Day magazine claimed the 42-year-old Sunrise presenter has been secretly dating aviation tycoon Paul O'Brien for several months.

Paul, who is believed to be worth millions, was previously married to Brisbane-based Ten Eyewitness News presenter Georgina Lewis, but they separated earlier this year.

Samantha and Paul were allegedly spotted having breakfast together during the AFL Grand Final weekend last month at the Crystal Club at Crown Towers, Melbourne.

'They looked very happy together at Crown,' a source told the magazine.

She was also spotted walking her dog with Paul in Sydney in March this year.
