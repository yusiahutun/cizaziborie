Cheeky boozer rebrands itself 'The Gym' to allow punters to ditch Dry January

Publish date: 2024-07-04

A cheeky Brit pub has renamed itself 'The Gym' to give its customers a guilt-free excuse to enjoy a pint during Dry January.

The Malsters Arms, in Swansea, Wales, made the switch last week, and photos show punters sipping from 'dumbbell' shaped glasses.

The idea is the brainchild of Au Vodka, the UK's leading premium vodka brand. Ben Marett, Head of Marketing Communications at Au Vodka, said: "Following the antics of New Year a lot of people have promised themselves, and their partners, that 2024 is the year they'll go to the gym more regularly."
