30 Facts about the movie The Blues Brothers

Publish date: 2024-02-02
Source: Nerdist.com

The Blues Brothers is a classic musical comedy film that has captivated audiences since its release in 1980. Directed by John Landis, this cult classic follows the hilarious and adventurous journey of Jake and Elwood Blues, played by John Belushi and Dan Aykroyd, as they reunite their band to save an orphanage from foreclosure.

Featuring an all-star cast, incredible musical performances, and a unique blend of comedy and action, The Blues Brothers has become a beloved film that continues to entertain audiences of all ages. In this article, we will explore 30 fascinating facts about this iconic movie, from its origins and behind-the-scenes stories to the impact it has had on popular culture.

Key Takeaways:

  • The Blues Brothers is a classic movie with awesome music and crazy car chases. It inspired a whole band and even a sequel! It’s a must-watch for music and comedy fans.
  • The movie’s impact goes beyond the screen, influencing fashion, music, and popular culture. It’s a fun mix of comedy, action, and amazing musical performances that will leave you entertained and inspired.

FAQs

1. Who directed The Blues Brothers?

The Blues Brothers was directed by John Landis.

2. When was The Blues Brothers released?

The movie was released on June 20, 1980.

3. Who are the main actors in The Blues Brothers?

The main actors in The Blues Brothers are John Belushi and Dan Aykroyd.

4. Is the music in The Blues Brothers original?

The soundtrack of The Blues Brothers features a mix of original songs and covers of classic blues and soul tracks.

5. Are the car chases in the movie real?

Yes, the car chases in The Blues Brothers are real and are considered some of the most thrilling scenes in the film.

6. What is the plot of The Blues Brothers?

The Blues Brothers follows Jake and Elwood Blues as they try to save the Catholic orphanage where they were raised by reuniting their old band and raising money through a series of performances.

7. How did The Blues Brothers perform at the box office?

Although The Blues Brothers initially received mixed reviews, it became a box office success and has since gained a cult following.

8. Does The Blues Brothers have any notable cameos?

Yes, The Blues Brothers is known for its numerous cameos, including appearances by musicians such as James Brown, Aretha Franklin, and Ray Charles, among others.

9. Is The Blues Brothers a comedy or a musical?

The Blues Brothers is a unique blend of comedy, music, and action, making it difficult to categorize it into one specific genre.

10. Can you recommend any other movies similar to The Blues Brothers?

If you enjoyed The Blues Brothers, you might also like movies such as “Animal House” (1978), “Ferris Bueller’s Day Off” (1986), and “Ghostbusters” (1984), which feature similar comedic and musical elements.
