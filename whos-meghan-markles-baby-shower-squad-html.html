Who's who in Meghan Markle's baby shower squad?

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Unseen photos from inside Meghan Markle's 'magical' baby shower reveal how the Duchess was surrounded by a squad of close friends, including tennis ace Serena Williams and Suits co-star Abigail Spencer.  

The Duchess of Sussex, 41, celebrated the impending birth of her firstborn son with the prince by flying to the Big Apple by private jet in 2019. Around 20 friends attended the five-day celebration.

In the fourth episode of the docu-series, which was released today, the royal mother-of-two shared a number of pictures taken with her friends, from the event, which took place in one of the most expensive suites at The Mark Hotel in Manhattan.

A group photo also features her university friend Lindsay Roth, makeup artist Daniel Martin and hair stylist Serge Normant. 

Left to right: Hair stylist Serge Normant, Soho House global membership director Markus Anderson; Suits star Abigail Spencer; fashion publicist Celine Khavarani; makeup artist Daniel Martin; mother-to-be Meghan Markle, designer Misha Nonoo; tennis star Serena Williams; fitness guru Taryn Toomey, and university friend Lindsay Roth.

In the snaps she can be seen beaming with friends, including fashion designer Misha Nonoo, fitness guru Taryn Toomey, and publicist Celine Khavarani. 

Speaking in the show, Meghan said: 'My friends surprised me with a really beautiful baby shower in New York. 

'They were like, "We're going to shower her with love, and shower her baby, and she's going to survive this and we're going to get through it with her."'

Meanwhile her friend Silver Tree said: 'It's like this really magical time of us just celebrating her.'

Serena said: 'It was just so cool and so fun to try to have an intimate moment.'

In one photograph Meghan, dressed in a navy blue polka-dot dress, can be seen laughing with her friends  

In one previously unseen snap, Meghan stood alongside Amal Clooney and Serena Williams as they take part in a flower arranging class 

Another snap from the event shows the group of friends all reaching out to Meghan's blossoming baby bump 

Other guests attending the event included Meghan's makeup artist Daniel Martin, Markus Anderson and her former BFF Jessica Mulroney. 

The shower was hosted by Serena, Jessica and Genevieve Hillis, who has been Markle's best friend since the age of 17 when they met at Northwestern University in Illinois. 

There were many guests at the shower in Manhattan, with reports that actor Priyanka Chopra also attended. 

Not only did the unseen photographs reveal Meghan's star studded address book, but also showed her dressed in a smart navy blue polka-dot dress.
