Zendaya and Tom Holland 'talk about marriage' but find dating in spotlight 'uncomfortable'

Publish date: 2024-05-19

There has been “talk of marriage” between Zendaya and Tom Holland, a source has revealed, but privacy has always been key.

Tom Holland and Zendaya are Hollywood‘s favorite power couple, and fans got their first double dose of the pair in the Marvel blockbuster Spider-Man: Homecoming in 2017. The pair first met on the film’s set and would go on to star in two sequels together, Far From Home and No Way Home. There were rumors about the pair being romantically linked from the start, but things only became officially confirmed in 2021. They’re still going strong, and that strength could lead to Tom on bended knee sooner than we think…

There has been ‘talk of marriage’ between Zendaya and Tom Holland

Fans smitten with the celebrity couple have been hoping to hear wedding bells for quite some time, and the two tying the knot is becoming more and more of a possibility.

“There has been talk of marriage, and that is a reality,” a source recently revealed to PEOPLE.

“They are not the kind of stars who put their lives together out there on social media for the most part,” the source included, suggesting that it could be some time before fans discover the big news, in the event they do become engaged.

“Both are private,” the source added, highlighting that widespread attention on their love life “has never been easy or comfortable for either of them.”

That being said, the two 27-year-olds are amongst the most popular actors working today, with Zendaya having a particularly big year thanks to movies like the sci-fi epic Dune: Part Two, as well as the steamy sports drama Challengers, in theaters from this Friday. Fans feel an investment in them that extends to their romance, and there’s perhaps nothing their adoring audiences would love more than to see them happily wed.

They want to keep the relationship ‘as sacred as possible’

During a 2023 interview with The Hollywood Reporter, Tom was asked about his life with Zendaya, but was quick to establish boundaries.

“Our relationship is something that we are incredibly protective of, and we want to keep as sacred as possible,” the celebrity explained. “We don’t think that we owe it to anyone, it’s our thing, and it has nothing to do with our careers.”

With the pair seen so often on screen by millions, they’ve admirably managed to keep details of their personal lives firmly under wraps.

“We were both very, very young,” Zendaya said of their meeting during a previous Vogue interview, “but my career was already kind of going, and his changed overnight… I definitely watched his life kind of change in front of him. But he handled it really beautifully.”

Married are not, fans are simply over the moon Tom Holland and Zendara are not only together, but thriving.
