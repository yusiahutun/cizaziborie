HUGE Lips are Back on Instagram!

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Earlier this week, Kylie Jenner stated directly that she loves the look of big lips, but not to the point where she’s had plastic surgery. Sorry, Internet.

Then, almost on cue, the paparazzi spotted her looking remarkably makeup free, showing that her natural lips have not been altered. Okay, Kylie wins.

Then, right after denying the plastic surgery rumors, Kylie posted more selfies featuring outrageously full lips for her 14.5 million Instagram followers:

The 17-year-old’s mouth was heavily drawn and painted over with dark lip paint creating a dramatic pout as she posed for the camera on Instagram.

Her first social media offering was a snap cropped so just Kylie Jenner’s lips and dyed blue curls could be seen with the simple caption: “gloooomy.”

Aww. Another featured the teenager in a crop top and beanie with black lace hanging in a veil over her eyes, with the caption “beanie by @monicarosestyle xx.”

 

Earlier this week, Jenner stated, “I think big lips are awesome. I feel like everyone has been talking about [my lips] for months, so I’m kind of sick of it.”

“But I love lip liner and I love over-lining my lips.”

Not all her fans heard her denials or saw the paparazzi pics to prove her point, apparently. “Enough injections please. it’s getting out of control,” one wrote.

While the Keeping Up with the Kardashians star dismissed claims that she had lip injections, blaming her love of lip liner, some fans remain unmoved.

Will they ever be satisfied by her denials?
