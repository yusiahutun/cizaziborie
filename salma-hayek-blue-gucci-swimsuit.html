Salma Hayek Embraces Mermaidcore in a Blue Gucci Swimsuit

Publish date: 2024-05-08

Salma Hayek just shared her most vibrant swimsuit look to date.

The Eternals star returned to Instagram to share yet another swimwear photo with fans. This time, she splashed around in crystal-blue waters while wearing a low-cut one-piece Gucci suit in a bright Tiffany blue. She wore little else to accessorize the look, but did carry a pair of sunglasses in one hand.

“Every now and then, your hair decides to cooperate when you least expect or when you least needed,” the actor wrote in the caption. She added a Spanish translation, writing, “De vez en cuando, tu cabello decide cooperar cuando menos lo esperas o cuando menos lo necesitas. #goodhairday.”

Hayek often takes to Instagram to post bikini-clad pictures of herself. Some recent posts include a plunging black one-piece, a sheer blue cover-up layered on top of a magenta bikini, and a boho patterned two-piece.

In a 2021 interview with Entertainment Tonight, she explained why she finds the act “liberating.”

“I had to lose a lot of weight and exercise to get into the bikini towards the end of last year. I’m glad I took a lot of pictures, I have no shame on it, because it was the first week of the vacation,” she said at the time, adding that she began to work out more often at the start of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020. “But after that first week and when I heard that everybody was going back into quarantine, I started eating again,” she joked.
