Leni Klum’s Father, Mother, and Full Name

Leni Klum is the biological daughter of Formula One driver Flavio Briatore and model Heidi Klum. She was adopted by Heidi’s second husband Seal after her mother Heidi had already broken up with him. Leni Klum Career as a Model, and Vogue Cover

Leni Klum aspired to be a successful model like her mother as a child. When she was about 12, she received an offer from one of her favorite brands, Brandy Melville. Heidi did not give her daughter consent because she did not want her daughter to go through the hardships and burdens of the modeling world at such a young age.

But, now that she’d turned 16, it seemed like she’d gotten the thumbs up from her mother’s side. In an interview with Vogue, Heidi stated, “Sixteen is a good age.” You can have an official Instagram account and show your face if you are allowed to drive a car.”

Aside from her appearance on a Vogue cover, Leni has spent a lot of time on the set of Germany’s Next Top Model, where her mother Heidi is the main judge.
