Victoria Beckham wows in micro blazer dress and heels for special family reunion

Publish date: 2024-06-05

The Spice Girl turned fashion designer relied upon her go-to silhouette for the occasion

    If Victoria Beckham says we should have it, consider us sold. The fashion designer and wife of David Beckham enjoyed a sweet family reunion on Thursday evening, where she met with her children Brooklyn and Harper, plus daughter-in-law Nicola Peltz Beckham.

    Once again turning to her trusty blazer dress look, the fashion mogul dazzled in the tailored piece which featured a micro cut, a double-breasted fit, a midnight black hue and devilishly sleek lapels. She paired the date-night number with some tights and heels, crafting the most VB aesthetic we could imagine.

    The 48-year-old wore her brunette hair down loose and subtly smiled for the camera, showcasing a rich makeup blend no doubt created with her own beauty products courtesy of her eponymous label.

    She embraced her husband for the image, who looked dapper in a classic navy suit and a crisp shirt. Beside her stood daughter-in-law Nicola, who turned out another colourful Y2K look. Despite wavering rumours of a family feud between the two Mrs Beckhams, the duo appeared to be in high spirits.

    Their acquaintance marked their second outing as a family, as the Beckhams came together to celebrate Brooklyn and Nicola's first wedding anniversary together last week.

    The couple tied the knot on 9 April 2022 at her parents Nelson and Claudia Peltz's oceanside Palm Beach estate. However, questions arose as to why Nicola opted for a custom Valentino gown instead of one made by Victoria's design team.

    Yet, their initial reunion proved to be a success – and not just in the style sense. Nicola oozed her signature Y2K aesthetic in the tiniest pair of micro shorts that gave Kate Moss' signature noughties denim hot pants a spring makeover. Victoria opted for an off-duty jeans and tee ensemble, serving up the perfect contrast to Nicola's playful aesthetic.
