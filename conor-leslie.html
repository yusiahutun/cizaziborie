Early Life

Talented actress Conor Marie Leslie was born on April 10th, 1991 in Millburn, New Jersey, the United States to her parents John and Rosanne Leslie. Her mother is a published author, motivational speaker, and intuitive life coach. Leslie grew up with her two brothers Dylan and Jack. Her family supported her wholeheartedly to pursue her career in acting. Along with acting she also has an interest in Photography and Philanthropy. Conor attended Millburn High School. She graduated as a junior in the year 2008 and relocated to New York City. There is not much information given regarding her education and qualification.
