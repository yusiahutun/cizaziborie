Early years

Even before turning four, Kristy had already started featuring in TV commercials alongside her brother Jimmy McNichol. Her first TV role was in 1973 in the show Love, American Style.

She later appeared on other shows such as Apple's Way, ABC Afterschool Special, Sara, Starsky & Hutch, and The Bionic Woman. As a teen actor, she once experienced attempted sexual abuse from the commercials director.

Her stint on the Family series

The young Kristy joined Family in 1976, where she played Letitia Lawrence. She was serious about this role and was always fast at remembering her lines. During this period, her mother also wanted to be an actress.

So the young actress was always trying to help her achieve the dream. Managing her career and her mother's made her even more exhausted.

More film projects, anxiety and depression

From 1978, the actress was already busy with movies. Two years later, she landed a role as Angel Bright in Little Darlings, a theatrical film. She also did a voice-over in Aladdin and the Wonderful Lamp animation fantasy film, playing the Princess's character.

Reappearance in Empty Nest

As expected of anyone in her position and status, rumours began spreading that Kristy's emotional state resulted from drug and alcohol abuse.
