Kris Lamb Named EVP And GM For Big Machine Records

Publish date: 2024-05-31

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) – Big Machine Label Group (BMLG), a subsidiary of HYBE America, is undergoing leadership changes with the appointment of Kris Lamb as the Executive Vice President (EVP) and General Manager (GM) of Big Machine Records (BMR). Lamb, previously the Senior Vice President (SVP) of Promotion and Digital, will now focus on the growth and management of BMLG’s frontline roster, which includes prominent artists like Tim McGraw, Carly Pearce, Midland, Aaron Lewis, and Jackson Dean.

Lamb will emphasize building and retaining the label’s lineup of artists in his new role, focusing on fan acquisition and engagement.

Scott Borchetta, Chairman and CEO of BMLG, expressed his confidence in Lamb’s capabilities, noting his 13-year journey within the label group. Borchetta commented, “There is an added pressure being the imprint that founded the company, and I’m thrilled he has accepted the challenge to take it even higher. Game On, Lamb Chop… GAME ON!”

Lamb expressed his honor in representing the notable roster of artists at the label and leading the forward-thinking executive team, saying, “I am beyond honored to represent this remarkable roster of culture-shaping storytellers and blessed to lead this team of forward-thinking executives at Big Machine Records. BMR has always been the tip of the spear, and I look forward to elevating our impressive roster of artists even higher as a team and impacting the future of this imprint, its talent, and the Country music genre.”

In conjunction with Lamb’s promotion, other changes within the imprint include the elevation of Brooke Diaz to National Director of Promotion and Marketing and Bill Lubitz to Senior Director of West Coast Promotion and National Strategy. Lamb praised Diaz for her multi-dimensional approach to promotion and highlighted the team’s commitment to guiding radio promo efforts to new heights.

The rest of the team comprises Director of Midwest Promotion Jane Staszak, Director of Southeast Promotion Jay Cruze, and Promotions Coordinator Sara Barlow. The collective changes and promotions within the leadership team reflect the label’s commitment to strategic growth and continued success within the dynamic music industry landscape.
