What is Fuerza Regida?

Fuerza Regida is one of the most popular Mexican pop music bands that has emerged in recent times. It currently has four members- Jesús Ortiz Paz, Khrystian Ramos, Samuel Jaimez, and José. All of them are youngsters and have earned immense reputation in a short period. It was initiated by Jesús Ortiz Paz, Khrystian Ramos, and Sammy Jaimez in 2015.

But it took two years to declare their existence in Mexico officially. Fuerza Regida is a social media sensation through which the band has built its base to get prominence. It is pretty recognized on social media platforms like SoundCloud and Youtube. En Vivo Puros Corridos, Del Barrio Hasta Aquí, Pisteando Con La Regida, Pisteando Con La Regida Vol. 2, Adicto, Otro Pedo, Otro Mundo, Navidad Con La Regida, Pisteando Con La Regida, Vol. 3 are the notable discographies by the boyband.

It has worked under record studios like Rancho Humilde – Lumbre Music and Rancho Humilde. Fuerza Regida has received as well as been nominated for several awards. In 2019, the band was set for the Premio Juventud for New Regional Mexican Generation award. The following year, it was nominated for iHeartRadio Music Award for Best New Regional Mexican Artist and Billboard Latin Music Award for Regional Mexican Album of the Year.

The pop group gained two nominations for the Lo Nuestro Award for Sierreña Song of the Year in 2020 and 2021. Fuerza Regida won the Billboard Latin Music Award for Regional Mexican Album of the Year. Its album Del Barrio Hasta Aqui was charted number one in the Regional Mexican Albums chart. The band has vast numbers of followers on social media too. It has 931 thousand followers on Instagram, 642 thousand followers on Facebook, 371 thousand subscribers on Youtube, and 20.6 thousand followers on SoundCloud.

Why is Fuerza Regida famous?

  • Fuerza Regida is a popular Mexican boy band. The group was created by Jesús Ortiz Paz, Khrystian Ramos, Samuel Jaimez, and José Garcia in 2015. It got official in 2017. The band released its first album in 2018 with the title En Vivo Puros Corridos. Soy Montero, El Fifty Pa Los Gringos, Fume Uno, Caminando Con Buen Porte, etc., are the most popular songs on the album. In 2019, the boy band released two albums namely Del Barrio Hasta Aquí and Pisteando Con La Regida.
  • These discographies received enormous appraisals from the audience as well as several recognitions. 2020 was the most successful year for the pop group in which they released five albums- Pisteando Con La Regida Vol. 2, Adicto, Otro Pedo, Otro Mundo, Navidad Con La Regida, and Pisteando Con La Regida, Vol. 3. Fuerza Regida also runs a Youtube channel that has 371K subscribers. It usually uploads its songs and music videos. The first song on the track is ADICTO – FUERZA REGIDA which has 354k views.
