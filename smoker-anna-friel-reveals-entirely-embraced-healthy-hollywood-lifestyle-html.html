Smoker Anna Friel reveals she hasn't entirely embraced a healthy Hollywood lifestyle

Publish date: 2024-02-29

She has been making every effort to embrace a healthy Hollywood lifestyle.

So the sight of Anna Friel smoking a cigarette on a photoshoot yesterday was surprising to say the least.

The former Brookside actress, who has permanently relocated to Los Angeles, recently hired a personal trainer in an effort to look the part in image-obsessed California.

Last week she was pictured jogging in the Hollywood suburbs while a personal trainer pushed her daughter along in a pram.

Yet such health awareness was nowhere to be seen yesterday as she took a furtive drag on a cigarette before posing for British Elle in Los Angeles.

Anna arrived at the shoot casually dressed, in a grey top and jeans, and was soon seen having a crafty smoke outside.
