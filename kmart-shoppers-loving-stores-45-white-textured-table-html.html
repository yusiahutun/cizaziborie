How to style a side table: 

Shape, height and colour must be considered when styling a side table: 

* Shape - Round side tables look good with simple objects, but square tables look better with big, curved items to make the straight lines less severe.

* Height - Try to vary the heights and widths of the objects on the table. Either a large piece of art work or a lamp will likely be the most prominent piece on a side table. Then coffee table books, vases or trinket dishes should be added for a variety.

* Colour - Choose bedside tables that complement the room rather than clutter it.
