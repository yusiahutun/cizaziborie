What Are Clear Backpacks For?

Clear backpacks are generally thought of as a safety measure for schools, music festivals, concert venues, and other large events.

What Is the Clear Bookbag Rule?

A clear bookbag rule is a policy that is being adopted by more and more high schools in the United States that requires students to carry clear backpacks only. The thought behind this policy is that students will not be able to carry in concealed weapons with clear and visible backpacks.

This policy has also been adopted at some festivals and music venues. Be sure to check the bag requirement rules and allowances before you attend a show to make sure you’re in compliance.
