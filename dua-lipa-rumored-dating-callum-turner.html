Dua Lipa Is Rumored to Be Dating British Actor Callum Turner

Publish date: 2024-02-18

Dua Lipa just danced the night away with her rumored new beau, British actor Callum Turner.

In video footage published by TMZ, Lipa was spotted slow dancing and seemingly sharing a kiss with Turner at the after-party for the Los Angeles premiere of Masters of the Air on Wednesday night. Turner stars in the Apple TV+ drama series alongside Austin Butler, Sawyer Spielberg, Barry Keoghan, and Lauren McQueen.

The clip shows Lipa, in a long black dress with a back cutout, wrapping her arms around Turner’s neck. He wears a plain black suit. The two sway softly from side to side before walking away with their arms still wrapped around each other.

Turner is a seasoned actor, having appeared in movies like The Boys in the Boat, Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore, Emma., and The Last Letter From Your Lover. His last known relationship was with The Crown star Vanessa Kirby, whom he dated for four years before the two called it quits in February 2020.

Meanwhile, Lipa was most recently linked to French director Romain Gavras. The two made their couple’s debut last May at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival, where she accompanied him on the red carpet for the premiere of Omar La Fraise (or in English, The King of Algiers). Lipa and Gavras maintained a relatively low-key relationship, with him occasionally making a cameo on Lipa’s Instagram.

“Dua and Romain are a great match because they both push each other to do better,” a source told Us Weekly in September. “They are both very creative people and have lifted each other’s art up to a new level.” Rumors of the couple’s breakup emerged last month.
