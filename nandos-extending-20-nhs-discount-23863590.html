Nando's extends 20% NHS discount to click &amp; collect orders

Publish date: 2024-06-24

Although restrictions are starting to ease, the UK is still feeling the effects of the pandemic, especially those who have been working on the frontline.

As a further thank you to the UK’s health workers, Nando’s has announced it will be extending its current eat-in NHS discount to cover Click & Collect orders for the first time.

This updated perk allows NHS workers to take advantage of their 20% discount on any orders made online for takeaway. The discount is eligible for orders £20 or more and will also be available when doors reopen to the public for dine-in later this month.

To access the extended discount is simple, NHS workers will need to head to the Nando’s website where they can begin a simple NHS email verification process.

Once complete, you'll be able to use your discount immediately, or whenever you feel the urge to satisfy your Peri chicken cravings via the Click & Collect option. You can sign up for further information online and find the full terms and conditions on the deal.

Nando’s has a history of supporting the UK’s health workers through its 20 year old in-restaurant discount, and more recently with food donations throughout the pandemic.

We might have to wait a little while longer to enjoy our Nando's favourites inside, but from April 12 some restaurants will be reopening for outdoor al fresco dining - which is equally as exciting with the expected warmer weather.

Whether it's for a Click & Collect order, or you'd like to plan dinner for six in the coming weeks - we've included this handy store locator tool to help you find your nearest Nando's branch. Don't forget you can order Nando's via Deliveroo, as well as for Click & Collect via the Nando's app or through the website.

It isn't just Nando's getting involved and giving back to NHS Heroes either. Many retailers from food, to fashion and everyday essential stores are offering select offers for NHS shoppers. We've listed a few worth checking out below.

Restaurants offering NHS discounts

  • Mindful Chef - 30% off recipes boxes
  • Las Iguanas - 50% off food to NHS workers at locations that remain open for takeaway and a free Coke can
  • Uber Eats - 10% off all orders & free delivery over £20 by joining the NHS Uber Eats corporate account
  • YO! Sushi - 50% off all food orders
  • LEON - 30% to NHS and all emergency services

To access some discounts you'll need to register online using a simple sign in form, for others you might need to insert a Blue Light Card number.

If you haven't got one already, it's free to sign up for one online. For use in stores you'll need a physical card, which costs £4.99 and lasts for two years.
