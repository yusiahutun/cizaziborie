Who are Vicente Fernndez grandchildren, and where are they now?

Publish date: 2024-03-23

Vicente Fernández has gone down in history as one of the greatest exponents of ranchera music. His musical legacy still lives on. You will be surprised to know that his grandchildren have followed in his footsteps to pursue a career in the entertainment industry as either musicians or actors. But how much do you know about Vicente Fernández grandchildren?

Vicente Fernández does not need any introduction to the Mexican entertainment scene. He was a famous ranchera singer, actor, and film producer. Over the course of his career, he recorded more than 100 albums and contributed to more than 30 films. He won several Grammy Awards thanks to his outstanding performance as an artist.

Who was Vicente Fernández?

Vicente Fernández was born on February 17, 1940, in Huentitán El Alto, Guadalajara, Mexico. The names of his parents are Ramón Fernández Barba and Paula Gómez. Vicente had a deep passion for music since he was a small boy. At the age of 14, he started singing at weddings and restaurants.

In 1965, he relocated to Mexico to pursue his music career. The most significant break in his career came in 1966 when he got his first contract with CBS Records of Mexico. He recorded several successful albums with the company, including Soy de Abajo and Ni en Defensa Propia.

Vicente recorded more than 100 albums and contributed to more than 30 films. He has gone down in history as one of the best-selling regional Mexican artists of all time, having sold more than 50 million copies worldwide. His repertoire consists of rancheras and Mexican classics.

Unfortunately, the Mexican ranchera singer is no more, as he died on December 12, 2021. Fernández died of complications from injuries he sustained when he fell at his ranch. He had also been diagnosed with Guillain-Barre Syndrome. Fernández was 81 years old at the time of death.

1. Alex Fernández

Alex was born on November 4, 1993, in Mexico City. He is the son of Alejandro Fernández and his ex-wife America Guinart. Alex is 30 years old as of 2023. He is one of the few grandchildren who have continued with the musical legacy of their grandfather.

2. Emiliano Fernández

Emiliano is the son of Alejandro Fernández and Xiemana Diaz. The young man prefers to lead a private life away from the spotlight, so not much is known about him.
