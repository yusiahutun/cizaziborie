Career and Progression:

Max Joseph directed his first documentary ‘State of the Economy: Oil Addiction’ in 2008. He has directed around a dozen films which include, ‘2 Years of DFA: Too Old To Be New’, ‘Garden of Eden,' ‘Let's Harvest the Organs of Death Row Inmates’ and ‘ Too New To Be Classic.'

Moreover, he serves as the co-host and cinematographer of hit television show, ‘Catfish: The TV Show’ which aired from 2012. The show earned him a nomination for Choice TV Personality: Male in Teen Choice Award 2014. He has edited and directed the commercials for Nike, Starbucks, Pepsi and Toyota Prius.
