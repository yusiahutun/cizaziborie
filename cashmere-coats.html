The cosiest cashmere coats to shop now and love forever

Publish date: 2024-04-04

Daniel Zuchnik//Getty Images

Burberry

An undeniable fashion classic, the Burberry trench coat deserves a spot in every well-curated wardrobe. Thinking of investing? Consider this pure cashmere iteration of the elegant Kensington design: sure, it's a little less practical, but it's a lot more luxurious.

Bottega Veneta

Bottega Veneta

Bottega Veneta offers up a modern take on the cashmere coat, without it feeling too trend-led. The clean, collarless design is the perfect hybrid between a winter coat and blazer, ideal for those trans-seasonal days that can be tricky to style.

Kiton

Kiton

Now 30% Off

An Italian luxury mainstay, Kiton crafts the classics with sublime attention to detail. This cream cashmere trench coat features a long, slender silhouette that's belted with an elegant tie at the waist. Nothing says 'quiet luxury' louder.

Cos

While cashmere tends to have a larger price point, there are some great brands offering more affordable coats this season. Turn to high-street favourite Cos for this wrap coat in a rich brown – one of this season's most popular shades.

Max Mara

We all know that Max Mara does great coats. Whether it's the fuzzy Teddy Bear style or a classic camel piece, you can't go wrong when investing in this timeless brand. Mix things up with this light blue hue; it makes a refreshing change from the staple neutrals, while still being very wearable.

Gobi Cashmere

Gobi Cashmere

Now 10% Off

Every timeless wardrobe needs a good black coat. Gobi Cashmere will serve you well year after year; its pieces are crafted from the highest quality cashmere, so they are designed to last forever. Better yet, the brand also offers plenty of cashmere accessories to add interest.

Joseph

Joseph

Now 30% Off

Joseph's take on the staple cashmere coat is one for those who prefer a more relaxed style, featuring an A-line fit and roomy sleeves ideal for layering. Crafted from a blend of soft wool and cashmere, it comes in three chic shades to choose from.

Toteme

We can easily see why this is Toteme's 'signature' coat. Featuring an oversized silhouette and a draped collar, it will add a luxury feel to whatever you wear.

Saint Laurent

Saint Laurent

Brown was all over the autumn/winter 2023 catwalks and now it's about to become central to your seasonal wardrobe. Saint Laurent's deliciously chocolatey cashmere coat oozes cool-girl style, thanks to its bold shoulder pads. An excellent statement-making investment piece.

Lunaria Cashmere

Lunaria Cashmere

Sometimes your outfit calls for a coat that's a little more on the casual side. Lunaria Cashmere's collared zip-up jacket strikes the right balance between smart and casual (and cosy).

Loewe

Loewe's cashmere coat is pleasingly long, ideal for keeping the colder temperatures at bay. Better yet, it also features a hood for an extra layer of warmth to get you through the winter season.

Theory

Theory

Now 45% Off

While neutral hues of beige and grey seem like the obvious choice when it comes to versatility, Theory's pastel pink is so subtle that it will work just as hard in your wardrobe.
