Simple cornrow braid hairstyles for natural hair

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Cornrow braid hairstyles have existed for many years and have since become one of the best protective styles among African women. Hence, cornrows offer a practical fun way to wear natural hair as they bring in style to the hair you are growing out. They also guard your hair from damage that's popular with other styling methods. As you gain experience in braiding, you will learn to separate the rows in various unique ways to achieve that cornrow appearance that is fit for you. From twisted to braided, thin to thick, and in an array of colours, here are several creativity options of setting your cornrow braid hairstyles for natural hair.

The practice of braiding offers a valuable means of strengthening friendship and familial bonds and sharing cultural expression. It also gives the cornrow braid fashioner a sense of pride for being an exceptionally special gift to others. This article highlights top 6 simple cornrow braid hairstyles for natural hair with the aid of well-selected cornrow braid hairstyles pictures to clearly bring out the various styles.

2. Jamaican cornrow braid hairstyle

If you have ever spend a vocational holiday in Jamaica, you might have noticed that most Jamaicans; females and males alike, have small, tight braids popularly known as Jamaican cornrow braid hairstyle. This eye-catching hairstyle is easy and affordable to make since you can do it perfectly with your natural hair. Though cornrow braid hairstyle is believed to have originated in Africa, it has remained a common style on the island and has taken an ethnic and symbolic appeal to the extent of attracting enthusiasts from various cultural backgrounds.
