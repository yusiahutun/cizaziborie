10 DIY 'Yellowstone' Halloween Costumes to Wear in 2023

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Longtime Yellowstone fans are waiting for season 5 to return with its final episodes. Due to the writers' and actors' strikes, the show is unlikely to return this year, but the Dutton family drama has garnered new fans after its debut on CBS. The popular show is filled with fashionable characters, ideal for Halloween costumes. The series brought a revitalization of the Western wear trend, and popularized cowboy boots, hats, and all things Wrangler. That should make these costumes easy to put together: You likely have some of the elements in your own closet.

Even guys who don't like dressing up are sure to find something they'll wear on this list. Who doesn't like to dress like a cowboy for a night? Rip Wheeler and Beth Dutton would make the ultimate couples costume, or you could grab your friends and put together a show-stopping group costume. You might even keep the party going and host a costume watch party for the CBS broadcast on Sunday nights.
