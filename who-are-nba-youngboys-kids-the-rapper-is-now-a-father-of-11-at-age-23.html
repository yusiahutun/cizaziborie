Who Are NBA YoungBoy's Kids? The Rapper Is Now a Father of 11 at Age 23

Publish date: 2024-02-15

NBA YoungBoy’s kids have grown in number again!

YoungBoy is a 23-year-old rapper whose real name is Kentrell DeSean Gaulden. The stage name, NBA YoungBoy, is short for “YoungBoy Never Broke Again.”

He is famous for many reasons, including his rap talent and the number of kids he fathered. He joined the big boy’s table in 2017 after Atlantic Records signed him.

Before that, he released mixtapes that received much attention on the streets. Since he rose to fame, YoungBoy has released two studio albums, one of which went to number one on the US Billboard 200.

How Many NBA YoungBoys’ Kids Are There?

NBA YoungBoy has a total of 11 kids as of April 2023. He shares them with nine different baby mamas. Some of NBA YoungBoy’s acknowledged kids include: 

Kayden 

Kayden is YoungBoy’s oldest child, born on July 14, 2016, and the rapper shares him with his first baby mama Nisha. He has appeared on screen in one of his father’s videos for the single “Kacey Talk,” along with his step-brother, Kacey.

Kamiri & Kamron

Kamiri is YoungBoy’s kid with Starr Dejanee. They welcomed him on July 6, 2017. Kamiri also has a sibling named Kamron, thought to be the rapper’s child too. Unfortunately, in June 2018, YoungBoy revealed on Facebook that one of his sons, Baby K, is not his biological child.

He wrote:

“Wanna Be First To Tell Yall I Officially Found Out Baby K Isn’t My Child, But The Man I Am I’m Going To Keep Raising Him Like He’s Mine I Cant Lie It Hurt To Find Out The Truth But Life Goes On.”

Taylin

Taylin was welcomed on March 19, 2017, by a woman named Nia and YoungBoy. The pair allegedly had a one-night stand, leading to Tayllin. He was confirmed to be the rapper’s son after a paternity test.

Kacey

Kacey was born on February 13, 2019. YoungBoy shares him with social media influencer Jania Bania.

Armani

Armani is the second child YoungBoy shares with his baby mama Nisha. She was born in 2020, and Kayden is her older brother.

Kodi Capri

YoungBoy welcomed Kodi with Houston actress Drea Symone on Thanksgiving 2020. Some rumors claim she is their second child.

Kentrell Jr.

Kentrell Jr is one of YoungBoy’s youngest kids. He was born on January 9, 2021, to the rapper and Iyanna Mayweather, the daughter of boxer Floyd Mayweather. In April 2020, Iyanna got arrested for allegedly stabbing one of the other NBA YoungBoy’s baby mamas.

Alice Nora 

Nora is YoungBoy’s first child with his wife, Jazlyn Mychelle Hayes. She was born in 2021. 

Klemenza Tru 

Klemenza is YoungBoy’s second child with his wife, Jazlyn Mychelle. She was born in September 2022, making the rapper a proud father of ten.

NBA YoungBoy Welcomed His 11th Child in 2023

YoungBoy welcomed his 11th child with his ninth baby mama in April 2023. After news of the child spread on social media, fans immediately went on the hunt for the mother, and it did not take long before she was found. 

The rapper shares the baby with a model named Drew Valentina. Back in November 2022, Valentina shared a picture of her baby bump. In her captions, she thanked the child for choosing her to be the mom but said nothing about the father. Fans flooded the comment section with questions about who he could be, and one user asked: “So NBA YoungBoy the daddy?” 

Valentina made no replies, but after news of YoungBoy’s new child spread, it was noticed that Valentina also lost her baby bump. A fan page dedicated to the hitmaker has also stated that Valentina recently welcomed a child, and her Instagram pictures corroborate this.

Details, including what YoungBoy and Valentina will name the child, are unknown, but fans won’t have to wait long, given how much the proud parent enjoys showing off his kids.
