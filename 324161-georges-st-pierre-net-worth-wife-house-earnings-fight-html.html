Georges St-Pierre biography

The super-talented MMA fighter was born on 19th May 1981 in Saint-Isidore, Quebec, Canada. He was given the name Georges St-Pierre by his parents Roland and Pauline St-Pierre. As he was growing up, he took part in several sporting activities such as karate, skating, and hockey.

He engaged in other combat sports when he was 16 years old. These include Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, boxing, and wrestling. Before going pro at 21 years, St-Pierre also undertook several odd jobs to gain financial support. However, very little information has been given about his educational background.

St-Pierre began his professional career in 2001, and ever since, he has won several awards and nominations. During his first match, he defeated a highly ranked opponent by a clear verdict.

In October 2004, he got his first shot at the UFC 50 when he faced Matt Hughes at the UC Welterweight Championships.
