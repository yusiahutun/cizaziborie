Tom, Katie and Suri's Ice Skate Date

Publish date: 2024-05-21

Tom Cruise, Katie Holmes and little Suri Cruise prove that a family that plays together stays together – the trio took to the ice over the weekend for some family fun!

The Cruises enjoyed the chilly weather and visited an ice-skating rink in Pittsburgh on Saturday. The family has spent the last few weeks in the city while Tom is shooting the film “One Shot,” due to hit theaters in 2013.

Suri looked adorable in a gray dress and pink leggings, while mom Katie sported flare jeans and a winter-ready sweater. Papa Tom proved that he's still got it, looking good in a green turtleneck and fitted, dark jeans.

The happy family definitely had reason to party this weekend – Tom and Katie celebrated their fifth wedding anniversary on Friday.
