Top 7 Miami's Luxury Pre-Construction Condominiums| The Enterprise World

Publish date: 2024-03-09

(Source-luxlifemiami)

Miami’s skyline is evolving with pre-construction condominiums reinventing distinctiveness and redefining luxury. The cityscape has become a canvas for modern architects, reflecting their ambitions to push boundaries and create iconic structures. Here, we explore the architectural marvels that go beyond convention and contribute to the dynamic pulse of Miami’s luxury pre-construction condominiums. 

EDITION Edgewater Pre-Construction Condominiums 

Expected to be delivered in 2026, EDITION Residences Edgewater is a 55-story glass tower designed by the visionary Bernardo Fort-Brescia from Arquitectonica and developed by Two Roads. This new super-luxury waterfront condominium epitomizes modernism and stands as an icon of the city’s avant-garde aesthetic. The tower houses 185 residences, each meticulously curated by Studio Munge to blend modern luxury with the natural beauty of the Magic City. 

Nestled in the vibrant Edgewater neighborhood at 2121 North Bayshore Drive, the property features 45,000 square feet of amenities, including direct access to the Miami Baywalk, a grand three-story lobby, library, social lounges, rooftop pool, cinema, poolside lounge, fitness center, yoga studio, spa, and a multi-sport simulator.  

A full-service poolside lounge, fitness center, spa, and multi-sport simulator cater to residents’ well-being. Gourmet dining, children and teen rooms, luxury guest suites, and a boardroom complete the offerings, emphasizing a commitment to environmental sustainability with electric vehicle charging stations.  

Casa Bella by B&B Italia 

Another visionary structure by Arquitectonic is Casa Bella Residences by B&B Italia. Standing tall with 57 stories and 306 luxury residences in the heart of Miami’s Arts & Entertainment District, Casa Bella Residences boasts 6 to 7 units per floor, private elevator access, and captivating water and city views, offering a unique blend of sophistication and convenience.  

Arquitectonica’s avant-garde design, which has been pushing the boundaries since 1977, incorporates exquisite materials, geometry, patterns, and colors. Renowned architect Piero Lissoni lends his mastery to interior design, creating an ambiance of timeless sophistication. Casa Bella is a masterpiece in the thriving Arts & Entertainment District, surrounded by cultural landmarks, museums, and vibrant districts. It is one of Miami’s luxury condos that pioneer modern living.  

Bentley Residences 

Bentley Residences Miami, the first residential development carrying the prestigious Bentley name, is set to become the tallest residential building on any US beachfront. Located in Sunny Isles Beach, Florida, the 61-story building will offer 216 residences with breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean and Intracoastal waterways.  

Scheduled for completion in 2026, the collaboration between Bentley, Sieger Suarez Architects, and Dezer Development emphasizes a fusion of indoor and outdoor living. Each residence features floor-to-ceiling windows and expansive balconies with private, heated swimming pools. A distinctive feature is a patented car elevator, allowing residents to drive directly to their apartments via the “Dezervators.”  

The development prioritizes Bentley’s design language, attention to detail, and sustainability, incorporating locally sourced materials and adhering to environmental certifications, such as the Florida Green Building Coalition. Prospective buyers can explore the project at the on-site sales gallery in Sunny Isles Beach. 

The Shore Club Miami 

The Shore Club Condos, an 18-story oceanfront tower in South Beach, boasts a stunning architectural design by Robert A.M. Stern and Kobi Karp. Inspired by ocean waves, the building’s rounded exterior and nautical-themed interiors feature oak woods, nickel and brass accents, and arched windows.  

The condos, offering a prime location, provide wraparound balconies with breathtaking views of the ocean, South Beach, and the Miami skyline. The Shore Club positions residents in the heart of South Beach, granting access to the best of South Florida, including beaches, restaurants, cultural venues, shopping, and nightlife.  

In terms of amenities, the condos include a cafe, wine and beer bar, coworking space, valet parking, pool, jacuzzi, and a fitness center. The units feature spacious floor plans, 10-foot ceilings, floor-to-ceiling windows with ocean views, and well-designed kitchens with sleek cabinets and gourmet stainless steel appliances. The Shore Club offers a world of luxury for its residents, combining a vibrant location with resort-style living. 

The St. Regis Brickell Residences 

The St. Regis Residences in South Brickell’s Miami, designed by Robert A. M. Stern Architects, is a 48-story waterfront tower with 152 extra-large, ultra-luxury pre-construction condominiums. Strategically positioned for expansive views, the residences boast a design that evolves skyward, featuring setbacks with private terraces and undulating curves reminiscent of the bay’s waves. 

Notable amenities include a private marina, outdoor and indoor pools, a spa, a beauty salon, Enzo Enea-designed grounds, golf simulator, teen video game lounge, a children’s entertainment room, pet spa, a luxury house car, and more. The two-to-seven-bedroom homes boast unobstructed views of the Miami skyline, Biscayne Bay, and the Atlantic Ocean.  

Residents enjoy 24/7 concierge, valet parking, EV charging stations, and St. Regis butler service, providing a modern take on the Gilded Age butler service from the original New York City hotel. Exclusive privileges include membership to the St. Regis inner circle, a private beach club, and global access to the brand’s renowned properties.  

Ella Miami Beach 

As a tribute to the iconic Ella Fitzgerald, Ella Miami Beach introduces a sophisticated residential experience with 95 exclusive condos that break free from rental restrictions, embodying the glamour of Miami Beach’s golden era. This 9-story architectural marvel by Arquitectonica sets a new benchmark in Miami’s real estate landscape.  

The luxury pre-construction condominiums, ranging from 1 to 2 bedrooms, boast breathtaking ocean and bay views, complemented by abundant appliances, closets, and fixtures. Arquitectonica’s avant-garde approach to design, characterized by inventive use of materials, geometry, pattern, and color, imparts a humanistic modern touch. 

The development trio, Constellation, The Boschetti Group, and Vietmar, brings over 30 years of expertise in large-scale real estate transactions, ensuring Ella Miami Beach stands as a testament to their commitment to excellence in real estate development. 

Five Park Miami Beach 

Five Park is an upcoming 48-story condominium building in Miami Beach, set to be the city’s tallest tower at 518 feet upon completion in 2024. Developed by Terra Group and GFO Investments, designed by Arquitectonica, and featuring interiors by Gabellini Sheppard, the building will house 98 condo residences ranging from 2 to 5 bedrooms.  

Located at 500 Alton Road, Five Park offers proximity to Miami Beach’s shopping, dining, and Flamingo Park. The apartments will boast private elevators, open floor plans, 10-foot-high ceilings, custom kitchens, finished terraces, stone countertops, and Gaggenau appliances, providing views of the Miami skyline, Biscayne Bay, and the Atlantic Ocean.  

Beyond luxury pre-construction condominiums, Five Park provides an array of amenities, including a 24/7 concierge, co-working spaces, a children’s learning lab, a screening room, resident-only pool bar, a gym and spa, and exclusive access to the Canopy Club’s dining and wellness facilities. The building emphasizes sustainability and enjoys a prime location adjacent to the MacArthur Causeway entry to Miami Beach.
