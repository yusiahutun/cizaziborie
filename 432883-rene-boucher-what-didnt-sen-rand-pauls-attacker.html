Rene Boucher versus Rand Paul case ruling and sentencing

Boucher was found guilty and sentenced to 30 days in jail for the attack. He was also given 100 hours of community service and a $10,000 fine. However, prosecutors said that the sentence was too lenient and won the right to argue for a harsher one. As a result, he received an additional eight months in prison for the assault.

Boucher was also ordered to pay the Congressman about $582,000 in damages in a separate civil case. He raised $482,000 from selling his house. In addition, Rene Boucher's GoFundMe initiative was also set up, though not much has been revealed about it.
