Nyaribari Chache MP Zaheer Jhanda has left netizens in awe after flaunting his new expensive rides.

What are Zaheer Jhanda's latest cars, their price?

A video posted by @nairobigossipclub on Instagram shows Zaheer driving into his residence in a sleek black Range Rover Sport.

Another section of the clip shows more of Jhanda's impressive collection as he strolls towards a shaded area where the Range Rover is parked beside a new 2024 Mercedes Benz convertible.

About the Rover Ranger Rover Sport

Car and Driver prices the 2024 Range Rover Sport between KSh 11.4 million and KSh 16.1 million, depending on the trim and options.

This model is renowned for its comfort and luxury, rivalling the likes of the BMW X5, Porsche Cayenne, and Mercedes-AMG GLE53.

It boasts a range of powerful engines, from a 355-hp inline-six to a 542-hp plug-in hybrid.

The interior is equally impressive, featuring high-quality materials and two rows of comfortable seating.

Additionally, it comes equipped with an eight-speed automatic transmission, standard all-wheel drive, and an adjustable air suspension.

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz CLE Cabriolet, on the other hand, is a front-engine, all-wheel-drive, 4-passenger, 2-door convertible with prices starting at KSh 7.4 million for the CLE300 4Matic Cabriolet and KSh 8.5 million for the CLE450 4Matic Cabriolet.

According to Car and Driver, the CLE450 has a turbocharged 3.0-litre inline-six engine producing 375 horsepower and 369 pound-feet of torque, coupled with a nine-speed automatic transmission.

Meanwhile, the CLE300 features a turbocharged 2.0-litre four-cylinder engine delivering 255 horsepower and 295 pound-feet of torque. Both models include a 48-volt starter generator.

Zaheer Jhanda tables bill banning vape pens

Recently, MP Zaheer addressed the mass use of shisha and vape.

Seeking a statement from the Interdepartmental Committee on Health at the National Assembly, Jhanda said the use of shisha and vape is widespread in entertainment joints across the country.

Jhanda warned that the vice's proliferation in rural towns was threatening the lives of the youth and endangering the country's future.

The MP noted that the ministry must make a hard decision to firmly address the issue by outright banning the importation, sale, consumption or distribution of shisha and vape pens.
