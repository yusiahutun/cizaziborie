Her long blonde locks are one of her most distinctive features but Jenny McCarthy has gone for a drastic new look. 

The 41-year-old former Playboy model has ditched her extensions and unveiled a tousled bob as her new hairstyle after insisting she wants to be more 'authentic' in 2014.

However, the View panellist told her co-hosts she has no plans to remove her breast implants or stop getting fake tans despite her new motto.

New year, new you! Jenny McCarthy took to Twitter to share a picture of her new shorter hairstyle after first unveiling it on The View

Jenny unveiled the look on The View and said: 'I did this hairdo, by the way, because I was tired of having the extensions. I wanted to feel a little more authentic this year.'

After joking about removing her breast implants, she also admitted she wasn't ready to give up her fake tan.

When asked by her co-host Barbara Walters, 'So where did you get this tan?', she replied: 'The beautiful tan I got was from Times Square, from a spray - tan salon, Barbara.'

She later posted a picture to Twitter with the caption 'Glad u like the chop! Thanks!'

Last year Jenny ditched her extensions for a few weeks but couldn’t last without them.

Jenny is currently dating Blue Bloods star Donnie Wahlberg, who she met when he first appeared on her Jenny McCarthy show.

Drastic: Jenny cut her hair and ditched her extensions after deciding she wanted to be more authentic in 2014

'I wound up giving him my number,' she admitted to Bethenny. 'I made the first move.'

I think he would be an amazing husband!' she gushed. 'So, if he does [propose] yeah, I would sure say yes. I wouldn't be surprised if I was the one proposing!'

Initially, Jenny claimed she feared Walhberg wasn't interested in her, because he waited two weeks before calling to ask her out for the first time.

By sending Donnie her way, the universe answered Jenny's wishes. 

'I told the universe I'm ready for love,' she told Bethenny. 'I said bring the one. I'm not doing any more auditions.'

Her feelings for him are reciprocated. 

The 44-year-old actor told In Touch Magazine: 'Do I feel like she's the one? She's everything I could have ever asked for.

'We were both married, both divorced, both have kids and both never married again, so we're both open to see what happens.

'We're having a good time. We're both workaholics but luckily we have similar schedules so we've really been enjoying things.'
