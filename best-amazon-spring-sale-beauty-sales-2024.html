The 42 Best Beauty Deals to Shop Now

Publish date: 2024-07-30

Amazon just kicked off its first-ever Big Spring Sale 2024, and on top of rare seasonal discounts in nearly every category, its beauty deals in particular are not to be missed. But there's only a limited amount of time to snap up these massive savings. From now through March 25, the retailer's sale features discounts on hundreds of covetable products.

Shoppers can take advantage of up to 50 percent off on top skincare and makeup favorites from L'Oréal Paris, Strivectin, Tula, and more along with other routine essentials like Gillette's top-rated women's razors. And for anyone looking to try a bouncy salon blowout at home, we've even spotted some celebrity stylist-approved hair tools at half off the original price.

Here, we've pulled together all the best Amazon Spring Sale 2024 beauty deals worth shopping right now. And don't forget to keep checking back here–we'll continue to add even more great deals to this list as soon as they're released.

Best Amazon Big Spring Sale 2024 makeup deals

Amazon is one of our favorite retailers for scoring drugstore makeup at great prices. Right now, the e-commerce giant is offering nearly 40 percent off some of its highest-rated products, like L'Oreal's Voluminous Original Mascara and Maybelline's Fit Me Foundation. Additionally, you can shop Amazon's collection of premium beauty products, including Julep's waterproof eyeshadow stick and Laura Geller's liquid highlighter, for up to 38 percent off.

Best Amazon Big Spring Sale 2024 skincare deals

Amazon has an impressive selection of dermatologist-approved drugstore skincare products as well as luxury skincare items. Though you can currently enjoy discounts on both, you'll find steep savings on premium options from labels like Strivectin and Tula. Additionally, Amazon's bestselling K-beauty labels like CosRX, Innisfree, and Juno & Co. are offering over 50 percent off some of their top products.

Best Amazon Big Spring Sale 2024 haircare deals

Amazon's beauty price drops are teeming with noteworthy haircare products and tools. We're currently eyeing Briogeo's repairing shampoo and conditioner set, SheaMoisture's ultra-nourishing mask, and popular devices like Kim Kardashian's go-to curling iron and the viral Revlon Hot Air Brush for up to 50 percent off.

Best Amazon Big Spring Sale 2024 body care deals

When it comes to your everyday needs, Amazon is probably already the first retailer you go to. Apart from paper towels and your other household necessities, Amazon is offering discounts on your bathroom and shower must-haves—from softening body lotions to disposable razors. And to complete your self-care routine, consider snagging some Crest Whitestrips, a natural deodorant from Native, or Cuccio's best-selling cuticle oil for less than $10.
