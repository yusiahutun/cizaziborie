30 funny ways to ask someone out that will impress him or her

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Asking someone out can be quite intimidating; you don't want it to be too formal or feel too awkward. However, that doesn't mean you can't find creative ways to pop the question. Considering funny ways to ask someone out works magic. But which are the best and funniest ways to phrase the questions?

Using funny pickup lines and jokes to ask someone out is a playful way to show your intentions to your crush or partner without sounding weird or awkward. It is also a charming initial step that can easily win you more future dates.

Funny ways to ask a girl out over text

Using funny texts is one of the clever ways to ask someone out over text and help you avoid awkwardness in case she declines the invitation. Here are some examples that will make her smile and accept your offer:

  • Can we start a podcast titled Drinking Wine and Talking? Do you want to try it?
  • I'm tired of looking at my apartment and seeing the same things. Can you please come so that I can see something new?
  • I just ordered a take-out for a family of five and am alone. Do you want to join?
  • I'd want to ask you out, but I'm too selfish to let other guys see your beauty. Can you go on a date with me?
  • So, is your dog around? I'd love to take it on a walk with you.
  • I'm not a photographer, but I have pictured you and me together eating and drinking coffee.
  • Do I know you? You look like my next long-term girlfriend.
  • I thought your dad was a terrorist because you're a bomb.
  • Please pass again because I believe in love at first sight.
  • Would you like to read a book or watch a movie based on that book?

Cute and funny ways to ask for a coffee date

Asking a girl for a date may be nerve-wracking, but it's not impossible. Most girls are fans of coffee, and using some humour while asking her out for coffee is one of the funny ways to ask her out. Below are funny ways to ask someone out that will make her giggle:

  • I know a place that serves the best latte. Do you want to join me?
  • Hey, would you like to grab coffee sometime? I'd like to know more about your passion for photography.
  • You seem interesting, and I'd like to know that over coffee.
  • I discovered a new coffee shop. Would you explore it with me?
  • Let's go for a coffee together.
  • Would you like to go out for a coffee with me?
  • You know, there's nothing like a good chat over coffee. Would you like to join me over the weekend?
  • I know you're into spots. Let's check out this new coffee cafe together.
  • What do you say? We turn our lunch break into a coffee break.
  • I'd like to hear more about your travels. Coffee and stories sound like a good plan, right?

  • Do you want to go out for a movie?

  • Let's go out because I love listening to your lame talk.
  • I want to be your Uber driver. Do you mind?
