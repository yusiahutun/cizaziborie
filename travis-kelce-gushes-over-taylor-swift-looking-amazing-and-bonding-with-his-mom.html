Travis Kelce gushes over Taylor Swift 'looking amazing' and bonding with his mom

Publish date: 2024-08-11

After first being linked to one another back in July 2023, Travis Kelce is now singing Taylor Swift’s praises after she attended one of his games in September. The Kansas City Chiefs player went to Taylor’s Arrowhead Stadium concert a couple of months back and that’s where dating rumors began.

Now, the two haven’t confirmed or denied whether they’re dating, but he had a lot of good things to say about the Blank Space singer in his latest podcast episode. Travis was smiling from ear to ear as he and his brother and co-host, Jason Kelce, discussed his “not-so-personal life.”

Travis Kelce talks about his personal life

At long last, American footballer Travis is sharing details of his personal life with the world via his podcast, New Heights.

He and his brother sat down and addressed some of his feelings towards Taylor Swift during episode 55 on September 27.

After the music star showed up to one of his football games, he almost had no choice but to open up about his “not-so-personal-life” on his show.

Taylor made a good impression

Speaking on his New Heights podcast, Travis got to the segment where he was set to talk about Taylor.

He smiled: “We’re finally here, huh?”

His brother, Jason, said that they had been “avoiding this subject,” and alluded to a “special guest” who was watching his game in the Travis Kelce suite.

Travis said: “Shout out to Taylor for showing up, that was pretty ballsy.”

He added: “She looked amazing and everybody was talking about her in great light.”

She bonded with his mom

Taylor’s appearance at Travis’ game “broke the internet” in Jason’s words.

His brother joked: “How does it feel that Taylor Swift put you on the map?”

Travis opened up about what he described as a “perfect day for Chiefs fans,” adding that it was great to see “the slow motion chest bumps,” and “the high fives with mom.”

Taylor could be seen in Travis’ suite at the game, cheering his team alongside his mom, Donna Kelce.

The NFL star added that it would be a day he’ll “always remember,” and that he and Taylor “slid off in a getaway car at the end.”
