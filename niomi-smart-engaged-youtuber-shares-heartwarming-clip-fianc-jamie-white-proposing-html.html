Niomi Smart is engaged! YouTuber shares heartwarming clip of fianc Jamie White proposing

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Niomi Smart has revealed she has got engaged to her boyfriend Jamie White.

The YouTube star shared a heartwarming video to Instagram on Wednesday of the moment Jamie got down on one knee. 

The influencer thought she was going for a regular Sunday lunch at Jamie's parents house and was shocked when he proposed.

In the clip, Niomi can be seen saying yes to Jamie's proposal before he lifts her up in his arms while surrounded by their families.

Alongside the video, Niomi wrote: 'My heart feels so full I can't put into words how much love I feel ❤️.
