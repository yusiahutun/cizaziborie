Dick Van Dyke, 97, reportedly has his car keys taken away by 51-year-old wife Arlene Silver

Publish date: 2024-07-13

It may be the end of the road, quite literally, for Hollywood icon Dick Van Dyke, with sources claiming the funnyman's wife has taken away his car keys after a recent wreck.

The 97-year-old actor was left bloodied after he crashed his Lexus LS 500 into a gate in Malibu, requiring two stiches in his lip after slamming his face into the steering wheel, since the car's airbag did not deploy.

The actor was found bleeding from both the nose and mouth after the accident on March 15, and while he escaped with only minor injuries, Radar Online reports that his 51-year-old wife Arlene Silver has revoked his driving privileges. 

'Dick’s gotten to the point where he needs to be taken off the road — for his own safety and the safety of others,' said an unnamed insider.

'Arlene is terrified if she doesn’t take the keys away now, it may be too late!' the source added.

The actor is said to be not very happy with his wife's decision to take him off the road.

'Dick is chafing at Arlene’s concerns. He’s aware of his age, but he’s determined to maintain his independence,' the source claimed.

This isn't Van Dyke's first serious car accident, with another incident happening nearly a decade earlier in August 2013.

The actor was driving in his Jaguar with his wife when the car caught on fire, and both he and his wife had to be rescued by passers-by outside of Calabasas. 

While both Dick and his wife escaped unscathed, their vehicle burned down completely to the frame.

As for this latest wreck, Van Dyke reveals just days after the accident that he was feeling, 'fine' though admitted he was, 'sore all over.'

He added, 'The airbags did not deploy, so I just had a face plant right in the steering wheel and it just made me a little dumber.'

When asked about concerns for his health, Van Dyke shook them off, adding, 'I’m 97 — all my friends are dead.'

The comedy legend told fans he was still holding up, 'pretty good,' though sources claim Arlene doesn't want to press her luck, which is why she took his keys away.

'She doesn’t want to press her luck. She loves her husband very much and doesn’t want to see him die in the driver’s seat!' the source said.

The actor is still very active, as he was recently unveiled as the Gnome on Fox's hit reality series The Masked Singer.

He also opened up about his secrets to staying so youthful, revealing that his 51-year-old makeup artist wife has a lot to do with it.

'Genes, I guess, for one thing. Having a beautiful young wife half my age to take care of me - that works!' he said.

Van Dyke added, 'My positive attitude, I get that from my wife,' while also sharing his physical fitness regimen.

'And one other thing is, I wrote a book called Keep Moving: I still go to the gym three days a week and work out,' the actor said.

'And I advise everybody to do that, because that's what ages people — it's just a stiffening up and not exercising their muscles and their lungs. Exercise is the answer,' he admitted.
