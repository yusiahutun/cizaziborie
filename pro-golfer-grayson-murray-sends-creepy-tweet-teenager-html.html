Pro golfer Grayson Murray sends creepy tweet to teenager

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Pro golfer Grayson Murray has been busted firing off an inappropriate tweet to a 'pretty' high school girl before quickly deleting it.

The teenager from Dallas tweeted: 'Why does everyone hate on @GraysonMurray?' 

Murray replied: 'Idk but I hate the fact you are in high school. You are pretty.'
