Reese Witherspoon, 46, reveals what she eats for breakfast that helps keep her 25inch waistline

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Reese Witherspoon has an enviable 25inch waistline on her 5ft1in frame which helps her look sensational in designer gowns when on red carpets.

But staying so trim does not come without the effort, which the 46-year-old The Morning Show revealed in a social media post shared on Wednesday.

The mother-of-three shared a look at her breakfast which is far from bacon and eggs or a chocolate croissant. Her choice included a lot of greens to superpower her day.

Next to her toast was a glass of green juice which is typically made with celery, spinach and ginger.

She has said in the past that she likes Kim Snyder's Glowing Green Smoothie. 

And there was also a cappuccino on the table; the star has said she needs to start her day with caffeine. 

In 2019 Reese told Radio Times in the UK that she typically starts her day with no food.

'I just have a green juice and a coffee in the morning,' shared the Legally Blonde star.

Reese also shared images of her trip as she captioned the collection of snaps, '24 hours in DC to celebrate Women's History Month with dear friends and some new friends too!' 

Witherspoon, an Ina Garten fan, is careful with her diet as she loads up on greens every day.

She often has kale salad with some lentils or grilled chicken for lunch. Dinner is chicken or fish with dark greens.

'I don't believe in crash dieting or anything like that... I work out all the time anyway just to stay healthy,' she told UsWeekly. 

But she does have cheat days.

In a 2016 interview with InStyle, Witherspoon said once in a while she splurges with the Southern dish country fried steak and she will also order pasta when at an Italian restaurant.

Witherspoon also likes to exercise with her female pals like bestie Shannon Mabrey Rotenberg.

'I'm big into working out with girlfriends. It's an acquired skill, being able to discuss your love life, children, and friends — all while you're running. But we have mastered it,' she has said in the past.

And she often works out outdoors like running by her home in Brentwood, California.
