Rolex Arts Weekend heads to Mexico

Publish date: 2024-02-23

Picture: the Mexico City venue of the Rolex Arts Weekend

Over 150 artists will gather in Mexico City for a weekend in December, as part of the next Rolex Arts Weekend. The Centro Cultural del Bosque will host the event, which will feature dance, music, film, literature, visual art and architecture.

The event is held to celebrate the Rolex Mentor and Protégé Arts Initiative, now in its seventh cycle. Successful artists in various disciplines are paired with rising young talent for 12 months of creative collaboration.

Highlights of the weekend will include Booker Prize winner Michael Ondaatje in conversation with his protégé Miroslav Penkov to discuss their literary influences; the world premiere of a performance by the San Francisco Ballet; and recitals by a contemporary Mexican group, a Finnish composer and a mezzo-soprano former protégée.

The Rolex Arts Weekend is on from 5 to 6 December. For more information, visit rolexmentorprotege.com.
