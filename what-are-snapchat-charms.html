What Are Snapchat Charms?

Publish date: 2024-03-05

Although Snapchat's principal feature is Snaps, it isn't devoid of other fun and exciting upgrades. It brings fellow Snapchatters closer and lets them stay connected in real-time with Snaps, chats, and much more.

Based on these interactions between two Snapchatters, rewards come in the form of varying Snapchat charms. Here's all that you need to know about them and how to get them.

What Are Snapchat Charms?

Users were previously awarded Snapchat Trophies for performing different activities. However, they were discontinued and replaced by Snapchat Charms. Snapchat didn't entirely kill the idea of trophies but instead, put a different spin on it.

Whereas the trophies were a token of individual achievements, Snapchat Charms represent the relationship between any two Snapchat friends (or group members in a group chat).

Charms are private, available only for mutual contacts. They're not viewable if someone's blocked or hasn't accepted your request. Charms are also personalizedâ€” which is why there's a separate set of charms for each friend (or group).

How to View Snapchat Charms

Although Snapchat Charms don't serve much purpose or unlock special Snapchat features, it's always fun exploring how Snapchat has awarded you for hitting a Snap milestone with your friend. Here's how you can discover charms with another fellow Snapchatter:

Close
  • Launch Snapchat on your phone.
  • Swipe right to access all the active chats. Alternatively, you can access these via the chat icon on the bottom-left of Snapchat's home screen.
  • Now select a chat, preferably with a friend you talk to or exchange snaps with regularly. Tap the avatar to open their profile.
  • Scroll down to the Charms section where all charms are displayed.
  • You can scroll through the entire selection. Tap on a charm if you feel like reading its detailed description.

    • How Do You Earn Different Snapchat Charms?

    Snapchat rewards users when they fit specific criteria for the charm. Usually, the more you interact, the higher the number of charms you get, such as Snapstreak, BFs, and Snap Legends.

    However, a few Snapchat Charms pop up by default. For example, if you and your friend have enabled Snapchat's Birthday Party feature, all Birthday charms, Star Sign compatibility, and Astrological sign charms will be visible, regardless of how much you intereact.

    Different Snapchat Charms and What They Mean

    Some Snapchat Charms can be clustered together as they revolve around a similar theme. However, some are unique and deserve a separate category.

    Here's a look at some of the charms you can get on the platform...

    Birthday Charms

    Close

    Birthday charms are a great way to keep track of your friend's birthday, although there are better ways to see birthdays on Snapchat. Regardless, It's Their Birthday! charm signifies that it's your friend's birthday; Birthday Twins indicates you and your friend have birthdays less than seven days apart. If there's a six-month gap between your birthdays, you both are entitled to the Half Birthday Twins charm.

    Zodiac Signs and Birthstone Charms

    Close

    These charms are all about astrological signs, compatibility, and birthstones. If two people are born under the same sign, Snapchat awards them with It's a Sign charm. The Astrological Sign charm gives insight into your friend's personality and which unique qualities they possess based on their sign.

    Similarly, the Sun Sign Compatibility charm assesses compatibility based on both of your signs. It's a fun way to learn what the stars say about your friendship. The Friend's Birthstone charm illustrates your friend's birthday month with its respective birthstone. For example, a shimmering, blue Aquamarine stone signifies all March birthdays.

    This is just one of the ways you can enjoy the astrology profile features on Snapchat.

    Joining Snapchat Charms

    Close

    Snapchat's all about commemorating the beginning of user's journey on the app. It's why the app assigns users different charms based on the year they signed up.

    Snap OGs are those who have been using Snapchat since 2013, the year Snapchat launched Stories. But if you joined a year later in 2014, the same year chats were introduced, you get the Since Way Back charm. From 2015 onwards, users receive a Joined in 2015 charm, Joined in 2016, and so on.

    Snap Score Charms

    Close

    These are based on the average Snap Score between two Snapchatters. There's a different title for each level, and you'll get bumped up as your score progresses with increased interactions.

    If you're Snap Legends, you're already sitting atop the throne. Snapchat rewarded your regular app use with five stars and the top-most position in the hierarchy. Then comes the Snap Heroes charm bestowed to those who're beginning to use the app more enthusiastically, but their scores aren't high enough for the top level. They receive four stars.

    The next ones are the lower-tier charms: the Snap Masters charm (three stars), Snap Sophomores charm (two stars), and Snap Newbies charm (one star).

    Friendship Charms

    Close

    When two friends stay on the top of each other best friend's list consistently for two months, Snapchat rewards them with a Super BFF charm. This looks pretty cute but be wary of the Mutual Besties charm. The latter one only appears when their best friend is the same as yours, and so in a way, they might be competing for your BFF charm.

    In another case, two friends earn a Mutual BFs charm if they have a mutual best friend, although it doesn't show who the mutual Snapchatter is.

    New Friends Charm

    You'll receive a New Friends charm when you've recently become friends with someone.

    Keeping in Touch Charms

    These charms reveal the frequency of interaction between two Snapchatters. However, these are rough estimates as Snapchat provides no clear guidelines. If you've been friends with someone but never talked, Snapchat labels you with the Shy Guys charm, wanting to encourage a conversation.

    Close

    When you've lost touch with someone for over a year, Snapchat gives you an It's Been Forever charm to remind you to reach out. When it's just been a few months since your last interaction with a friend (six to 12 months), Snapchat serves you a reminder through the It's Been a While charm. It's Been a Minute charm pops up when it's only been a short while (six months or less) since the last correspondence.

    Close

    On the contrary, there are a few charms that depict frequent conversations between Snapchat users as well. If it's only been a week or two, you'll notice the It's Been a Second charm sitting in your charm list.

    But if you start interacting very frequently, it will get replaced by the In Touch charm. The Snapstreak, symbolized by a fire emoji, only appears when two Snapchatters exchange snaps daily without fail. The numbers on the charm reveal how long the streak's been going on.

    Snapchat Group Charms

    Group charms are based on similarities between group members and are visible to the entire group.

    Snapchat Employees Charm

    As the name states, only Snapchat employees receive a special badge of recognition that indicates they work for Snapchat.

    Charming Your Way Through Snapchat

    Keep looking around your friend's profile to track Snapchat charms you might've unearthed recently; this way, you'll be on the lookout for any new charms Snapchat might secretly introduce.

    Nevertheless, charms are only a fun product of Snapchat activity, nothing more. There's a lot more to Snapchat, especially if you want to learn how to navigate it like a pro.
