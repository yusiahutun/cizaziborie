Pregnant Kim Kardashian Goes Totally Naked -- Bump, Booty, It's All Here!

Publish date: 2024-04-08

Kim Kardashian is putting an end to some nasty pregnancy rumors ... and taking it all off while doing it!

The reality TV star took to Instagram today to share a completely naked photo, putting her baby bump, booty and everything else on display while shutting down reports she's faking her pregnancy.

"First they say I'm too skinny so I have to be faking it...Now they say I'm too big so I have to be faking it...SMH," she captioned the pic. "Some days I'm photographed before I eat & look smaller, some days I've just eaten & I look bigger. It's all a part of the process. I think you all know me well enough to know I would document the process if I got a surrogate."

"Everyone's body is different, every pregnancy is very different! I've learned to love my body at every stage! I'm going to get even bigger & that's beautiful too! I'm blessed to even be pregnant & even luckier to not have preeclampsia as far as I know, so I don't have the swelling issue this time," she continues. "They also say your body carries a boy different than a girl! Whatever the case may be I'm grateful to God for this miracle & no matter what rumors or comments you throw my way this time they truly don't affect me! #NoFilter #NoPhotoShop #GoodLighting"

And, despite her growing bump, Kim's not changing her signature style!

The expecting reality star stepped out on Monday, flaunting her growing belly while doing some shopping at Barneys New York in Beverly Hills.

The "Keeping Up With the Kardashians" star rocked a white and taupe color block midi dress, an Isabel Marant coat and a pair of Jimmy Choo braided rope sandals.

The 34-year-old has continued to favor figure hugging and sheer looks throughout her second pregnancy. Most recently, she's been a fan of covering up with long jackets and trench coats.

The sighting comes on the heels of little sister Kylie Jenner's big 18th birthday weekend, where the whole Kardashian/Jenner fam was spotted celebrating with the teen.

On Sunday, Kim and hubby Kanye West joined sisters Kourtney and Khloe Kardashian, Kendall Jenner and mom Kris at Bootsy Bellows for an over the top celebration in honor of Kylie.
