Zendaya Matched Her Plunging Polka-Dot Romper to Her Pointy Pumps

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Florals for spring? Groundbreaking or not, Zendaya just showed that being in full bloom is never out of style hot on the heels of the Spring Summer 2024 presentations wrapping up in Paris. In her latest Instagram post, the Golden Globe-winning actress and (never forget) Dancing With the Stars runner-up wore a flower-embellished Balmain romper that made its catwalk debut just a few days ago. 

In a pair of snapshots taken by photographer Ole Martin Halvorsen, Zendaya posed against a mirror wearing a super-short black-and-white polka-dot romper that plunged down the front. The deep V was embellished with 3-D leaves and flowers in bold, bright colors, and she finished the look with a pair of heels that matched the black-and-white, Cruella-approved color palette. And while she had been wearing her hair down and straight during her Paris Fashion Week appearances (most notably for Louis Vuitton, where she acts as one of the French brand's global ambassadors), the new shots show her with a short, curly bob. 

She captioned the post with a simple flower bouquet emoji.

The look could just be the icing on the cake of her Parisian fashion tour de force. After wearing a Louis Vuitton gown that was zipped almost all the way down to her bellybutton at the brand's runway show, she followed it up with a look that included archival LV pieces, like a Murakami rainbow-monogram Theda bag that was on everybody's wish lists back in the mid-2000s.

"I've always loved fashion," Zendaya told Vogue Italia last year. "I always loved clothes and they were in many ways a tool of expression. Fashion has taught me so much about myself and how to be so much more fearless in a lot of different ways. It has also helped me in other avenues of my life whether it be business or even as an actress to be more fearless and to not be so concerned with other people's point of view of what I look like or what I'm wearing."
