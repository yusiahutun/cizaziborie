EastEnders Jade death 'sealed' as fans floored after 'psychotic' Dean twist

Publish date: 2024-06-17

EastEnders fans fear Jade will die after Dean made a sinister move.

Jade Masood (Elizabeth Green) was reintroduced in 2023 as the daughter of Dean Wicks (Matt Di Angelo). It was revealed Jade was suffering with cystic fibrosis and she recently underwent a lung transplant.

During Thursday's episode of the BBC soap, which aired on Friday (March 1), Jade told Dean that she was moving to Pakistan with her mum Shabnam Masood (most recently played by Rakhee Thakrar). Although Jade admitted to enjoying life in Walford, she wanted to be close to her mother and grandma Zainab Masood (Nina Wadia) who had recently fallen ill.
