Keeping up with Bezos’ US$500 million megayacht Koru

If anyone can beat Pirates of the Caribbean’s iconic Black Pearl ship at its game, it is tycoon Bezos’ superyacht that will hence be known as one of the world’s largest and most ecological yachts. As the centibillionaire’s 470ft vessel Koru – named after a Maori word meaning “new beginnings” – gears for sea trials, the support vessel Abeona has completed them successfully.Almost twice the length of the Airbus A380, Koru boasts three enormous decks, a bowsprit, a colossal black hull and an elegant contour, according to NZ Herald. The three decks will feature amenities like an on-deck swimming pool, a cinema, several lounges and business areas spread across the enormous decks, reports the same source.
