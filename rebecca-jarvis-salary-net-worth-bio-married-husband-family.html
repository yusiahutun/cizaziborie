Bio, Wiki Of Rebecca Jarvis

Born Rebecca Ann Jarvis on 28th September 1981, Rebecca Jarvis is a journalist. Her birth sign is Libra. When it comes to her nationality, Jarvis is American and is of White ethnicity. She is the daughter of Gail Jarvis and James T. Jarvis. Regarding her father, he is a business consultant and a lawyer. Whereas her mother is a financial columnist.

Moreover, Jarvis earned HS Diploma in 1999 at St. Paul Academy. Similarly, she graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Chicago in 2003. Besides, she started her career in 2003 as an analyst in Bank of America. In 2006, she joined Crain’s Chicago Business as a writer. Similarly, from 2006 to 2009, Jarvis worked as a reporter on CNBC.

Moreover, Jarvis joined CBS News in 2010 as an anchor,  and business & economics correspondent. Since April 2013, she worked as an anchor, chief biz, and Tech & Econ Correspondent in ABC News. Besides, Jarvis also appeared in the reality show, The Apprentice. The hardworking journalist also has been nominated for several awards and won some of them including Emmy Awards.
