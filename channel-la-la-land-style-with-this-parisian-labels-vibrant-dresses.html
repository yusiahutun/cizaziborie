Channel La La Land style with this Parisian label's vibrant dresses

Publish date: 2024-09-19

Lionsgate/La La Land

Damien Chazelle's modern-day musical La La Land may be tipped as one of the must-see films of the year, but what about the fashion?

The Parisian label Tara Jarmon has been inspired by the Oscar-nominated film, designing an exclusive range of dresses that echo the bright outfits worn by Emma Stone's character, Mia and her friends in the movie. The frocks are available in four eye-catching colours: yellow, green, blue and red.

Courtesy of Tara Jarmon

The chic three-quarter-sleeved dresses – named after Stone's character Mia – all feature a criss-crossed waist belt detail and can be yours for £320.
