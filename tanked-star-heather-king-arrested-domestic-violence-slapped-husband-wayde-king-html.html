Tanked star Heather King arrested for domestic violence after she 'slapped' husband Wayde King

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Reality TV star Heather King has been arrested for alleged 'misdemeanor Domestic Violence'.

The Tanked star 'slapped her husband, Wayde, in the face and kicked him in the stomach twice during a heated argument' on Wednesday, TMZ reported.

She and her husband have co-starred together in the Animal Planet series since 2011.

When police arrived at the King residence, they 'noticed visible redness on the right side of Wayde's stomach' according to TMZ.

Wayde 'also told police when Heather drove away, he was hanging out the window of her car.'
