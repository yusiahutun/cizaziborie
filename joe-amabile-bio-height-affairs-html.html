TV personality Joe Amabile rose to fame following his appearance in the fifth season of Bachelor In Paradise (2014). He became a fan favorite after another contestant Becca Kufrin eliminated him in the first episode. 

He then went onto compete in the hit reality show, Dancing With The Stars, during its 27th season. In the show, he has partnered ballet dancer Jenna Johnson.

Joe Amabile's Past Relationship, Dating Status

Joe was previously in a relationship with Bachelor In Paradise (2014) co-star Kendall Long.

However, the same year, Kendall tried to patch up their relationship and asked for forgiveness by flying back to Chicago. She tried to get back together with him saying,

Every single day when I got back I was crying or thinking about you. But the reason I had so much fear is because I am in love with you.

They did rekindle their love again, and a year later, a new development came out on their relationship. In 2019, during an interview with E! News at iHeartRadio's ALTer Ego concert, his girlfriend hinted that they would eventually decide on getting engaged.

Split

Until 7th January 2020, everything seemed to be going fine between Joe and Kendall. They were still keeping their fans up to date on their whereabouts through Instagram. However, by the end of that month, the pair decided to call it quits and broke up.

The actor and his now ex-girlfriend even announced their split by releasing a joint statement that read,

We have decided mutually to go our separate ways. Joe has made the decision to move back to Chicago while Kendall will be remaining in her hometown of Los Angeles. Our family and friends have always been an extremely important element to who we are as people. We can’t imagine continuing our lives without them closer, especially when thinking of starting a family of our own one day. We both respect each others’ decisions and still have a great deal of love for each other. We appreciate all the love and support everyone has given us throughout our relationship.

The disclosed all of this through BachelorNation.com on 29th January 2020.
