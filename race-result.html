Race Result | 24 Apr 2024 | Happy Valley | Race 4 DAEGU HANDICAP | HK Racing

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Racing Incident Report

Horse No 1 MA COMET (H391) B Avdulla Near the 300 Metres was eased to avoid LEAN MASTER which shifted out and away from FORTUNE WARRIOR after being taken out by that runner.

Raced wide and without cover for the majority of the event.

Horse No 2 FORTUNE WARRIOR (H229) K Teetan Sent for sampling post-race.

Horse No 3 CHARITY FOR ALL (H417) K C Leung Slow to begin.

Near the 600 Metres when racing tight to the inside of STRIVE FOR GLORY was steadied and then ran out across the heels of STRIVE FOR GLORY and was checked.

Horse No 4 GRAND NOVA (J323) H Bowman After mounting H Bowman was dislodged in the Parade Ring without incident.

Jumped only fairly.

Near the 600 Metres was checked to avoid CHARITY FOR ALL.

When commencing to finish the race off well became held up for clear running behind GREAT ACHIEVER over the final 100 Metres and as a result could not be tested to the finish of the event.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 5 SERGEANT PEPPER (E432) E C W Wong Sent for sampling post-race.

Horse No 6 FAIRY HORSE (J144) A Atzeni Jumped only fairly.

Near the 500 Metres was steadied to avoid STRIVE FOR GLORY.

Horse No 7 STRIVE FOR GLORY (D186) L Ferraris Jumped awkwardly.

Approaching the winning post FAIRY HORSE (A Atzeni) improved into a narrow run between STRIVE FOR GLORY and MA COMET and then was taken in slightly when MA COMET was crowded by LEAN MASTER (H Bentley).

A Atzeni was advised to exercise more care to ensure that there is sufficient racing room when he improved into runs.

H Bentley was advised he must stop riding and straighten when his mounts shift ground when not clear to do so.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 8 GREAT ACHIEVER (J049) Z Purton After the race, Z Purton stated that his mount, which was racing in blinkers for the first time, over-raced badly throughout the early and middle stages and got its head up when being positioned close to LEAN MASTER.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 9 LEAN MASTER (H134) H Bentley Shifted in on jumping and was bumped.

Horse No 10 CIRCUIT SEVEN (D474) M F Poon Bumped at the start.

Raced wide and without cover for the majority of the event.

Horse No 11 GORGEOUS VITALITY (G051) L Hewitson Sent for sampling post-race.
