Gwen Stefani wows on AMAs red carpet in kooky dress

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Gwen Stefani made an eye-popping arrival on the red carpet at the American Music Awards on Sunday night.

The 46-year-old mother of three wore a kooky sheer fishtail dress that put her underwear on show. 

It also featured bat-wig sleeves and a thick black choker. She made a solo appearance on the red carpet despite her new romance with her co-star on The Voice, Blake Shelton.

Gwen will perform her new single Used To Love You at the ceremony. 

The emotional breakup song is believed to be about her split from fellow rocker Gavin Rossdale, who she shares three children with.

Gwen's appearance follows reports her ex had cheated on her with the family's longtime nanny. 

Breakup song: The No Doubt opted for an all-black ensemble for the event

While the singer has never outright confirmed whether the single is directly about her split, rumors heated up after a video was released of the blonde crying while singing the song in a dark room.

And the pop star appeared to confirm that her emotional new single really is about her ex when she recently appeared on The Today show. 

Gwen could not hide her surprise when asked if her new song Used To Love You was about Gavin and their recent split. 'Oh my god!' Gwen said visibly uncomfortable, 'Put me on the spot! I'm like, "Whoa" Whoa!"'

Sheer delight: The mother of three showed her lean pins in the transparent outfit

But she then admitted that the song was written recently and came from a deep personal place.

'I wrote that song a couple weeks ago,' she said appearing to confirm it was about her ex.

And regarding the emotional video that just came out, she explained: 'I sat in front of a camera in a dressing room randomly - not to make a video just to do some background video.'

'It's all very real,' she added.

Soon after another layer of subtext was added after reports emerged of Gavin, 50, being allegedly unfaithful to his wife of 13 years.

The revelations came after Gwen confirmed her romance with fellow The Voice host Blake Shelton, 39.

It has been a rough time for the blonde singer after she called it a day with the Bush frontman after he allegedly cheated on her with their nanny Mindy Mann.

Getting ready: The singer shared an Instagram of herself getting ready for the show

Last week, it was reported that Rossdale had been involved in a three-year relationship with the blonde Australian. 

According to Us Weekly, Gwen was forced to confront the British star when she discovered explicit pictures and texts on their children's iPad, which was linked to his phone.

‘The iPad was linked to Gavin’s phone,’ a family source told the publication.

‘One of the other nannies discovered the exchange and told Gwen.’

The admission reportedly ‘completely devastated her,’ an insider revealed. ‘She was mortified, livid, and embarrassed.’
