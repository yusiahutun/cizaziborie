The Best Memes from the Royal Wedding

Publish date: 2024-04-12

All eyes might've been on Meghan Markle and Prince Harry on their big day, but there were a couple of others who stole the spotlight once or twice throughout the royal festivities. From Prince George's outfit to the look on Prince Harry's ex's faces, many various viral moments didn't go unnoticed by the Internet. Scroll through to see the best memes from yesterday's royal wedding.

Prince George looked adorable in his matching frockcoat uniform of the Blues and Royals that his father and uncle also wore. The combination of his outfit and his reactions to the day's festivities reminding some people of some songs, movies, and tv shows that they can't get out of their heads.
