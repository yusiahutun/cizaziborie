Taylor Lautner has been letting fans say his name wrong 'this entire time'

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Everyone, hold up, we’ve just found out we’ve been saying Taylor Lautner’s last name wrong ‘for decades’. Maybe we weren’t as big Team Jacob fans as we thought. While we hang our heads in shame, we take a look at how you actually say Lautner, and why the 31-year-old hasn’t corrected us until now.

Taylor Lautner rose to fame after playing the dreamy character Jacob in the Twilight Saga from 2008. He and his costars quickly became worldwide phenomenons, but little did we know, we were pronouncing his name wrong this entire time.

Taylor Lautner reveals how to actually say his last name

The movie actor joined The Unplanned Podcast With Matt & Abby recently, and made a revelation no one was expecting, so how do you actually say his name?

“It is technically ‘Lowt-ner.’ But I just never, in interviews or anything, stopped someone and said, ‘It’s not ‘Lawt-ner,’ it’s ‘Lowt-ner,'” the star told listeners.

Although, no need to fret if you’ve been pronouncing his name wrong all these years, as the star’s not fussed. “I just go with whatever. I don’t care,” he revealed.

“It is my fault that nobody knows how it’s actually pronounced because I never corrected anybody for decades,” he continued.

But why has he let us say it wrong this whole time? Well, it’s actually because he was too nervous to correct anyone during his younger days.

“I think when I was 11, like after Sharkboy and Lavagirl, I wasn’t gonna tell people they were pronouncing my name wrong,” the star explained.

So to conclude, it’s Lowt-ner, not Lawt-ner.

Fans can’t believe they’ve been saying his name wrong

Of course, this huge revelation caused a stir on social media.

“OMG been mispronouncing Taylor Lautner’s last name this entire time…” said one Twitter fan.

“Taylor Lautner going on to a podcast and being like “…I’ve never corrected anyone on how to say my name properly” is SO ‘born on February 11’ coded,” joked one.

“Btw I found out Taylor Lautner’s last name is pronounced LAWTNER instead of LOTNER wtf I will not be pronouncing it that way ever I’m sorry I can’t bring myself to do it I tried I really did its just too much for me rn,” wrote another.

“When I tell u I gasped so f****** loud when Taylor Lautner said his last name is pronounced ‘lowt-ner’ and not ‘lot-ner’. I HAVE BEEN PRONOUNCING HIS LAST NAME WRONG FOR LIKE THE PAST 7 YEARS??? LIKE WHAT???”

However, it seems like not everyone has been saying it wrong as one fan tweeted: “I’ve been saying it correctly since new moon I care about you king.”
