Jax Admits to Cheating on Brittany and Everyone Picks a Side

Publish date: 2024-08-18

On Monday's episode of "Vanderpump Rules," Jax Taylor finally admitted to girlfriend Brittany Cartwright that the rumors were true -- he did in fact sleep with Faith.

At Scheana Marie's masquerade birthday party last episode, Tom Sandoval confronted Jax about the rumor he heard that Jax had slept with the former SUR server. While Jax was vehemently denying the allegations to everyone within earshot, Faith was busy telling James Kennedy about the night she and Jax allegedly got busy -- and that she might be pregnant with his baby.
