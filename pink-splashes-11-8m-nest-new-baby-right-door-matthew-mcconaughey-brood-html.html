With most new mothers-to-be, there is the inevitable nesting stage where they get everything ready for when they bring their new bundle of joy home.

And for pop star Pink, she has taken that a step further by splashing out $11.8million on a new home in Malibu, California to house her expanding family.

The 31-year-old singer and her motorbike racer husband Carey Hart have not picked any old spot to invest in - they have bought a home right next door to Matthew McConaughey.

Nesting: Pregnant pop star Pink has splashed out on a $11.8million home in Malibu, California just days before giving birth to her first child with husband Carey Hart

Impressive digs: The So What singer's new home is a large property within walking distance of the coastline and has a huge pool in the back yard

Family street: Pink's next door neighbour is actor Matthew McConaughey who lives at the property he bought in 2008 for $10million with partner Camila Alves and their two children Levi and Vida

The surf enthusiast lives at his $10million home that he bought back in 2008 with his partner Camila Alves and their two children Levi, two, and 15-month-old Vida.

Pink and her husband of five years are expecting their first child any day now and will no doubt be having play dates together.

Luckily, the songstress' new home has a large pool, which McConaughey's does not, so they can gather in Pink's back yard for a splash around with the children.

Roomy: Pink's house has a five car garage to house their many vehicles and motorbikes

Space to play: The large property is on several square feet of land with more than enough room for Pink's new baby to run around in

No shoes: The mother-to-be was pictured walking around Malibu Farmers market on Sunday literally barefoot and pregnant - she was grabbing a bite to eat

The Malibu homes has direct access to the beach making it easy for them to go for walks to the coastline.

With a five car garage, the property has more than enough room to house Hart's cars and motorbikes and has a guest house attached to the other side of the car port.

The large multi-level home is surrounded by lush greenery and is in one of the most desirable streets in the area.

Over the weekend, Pink was literally barefoot and pregnant and she walked around the Malibu Farmers market without any shoes on.

Wearing a long blue dress and a black cardigan, the So What singer who is in the final stage of her pregnancy obviously had comfort at the forefront of her ind and decided not to wear anything on her feet.

Happy family: Matthew McConaughey and his family are regular visitors to the nearby beach - he has been with Alves for over five years

She walked around the different vendors and picked up a bite to eat.

The Stupid Girls singer, real name Alecia Moore, says she has been eating lots of healthy food and sleeping for 12 hours a night to prepare for motherhood.

But despite her healthy effort, she doesn’t seem to be enjoying the natural diet and tweeted earlier this month: ‘fresh vegetable juice. Check. YUCK!’

Although the sex of their baby has not been revealed, the singer has said she thinks it will be a girl.

And Pink said of motherhood last month: ‘I have a life inside me, and I want her or him to know that I will accept him or her with open and loving and welcoming arms.

‘And although I will prepare this little munchkin for a sometimes cruel world, I will also equip this kid to see all the beauty in it as well.’
